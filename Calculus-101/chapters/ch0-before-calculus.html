Chapter 0. Before Calculus第 0 章 · 微積分之前

A precalculus review and diagnostic. These exercises rebuild the language of functions you will use all semester, so that limits, derivatives, and integrals rest on solid ground. They can be done as self-study; treat them as a warm-up before Chapter 1.微積分前的複習與診斷。這些練習重建你整個學期會用到的函數語言，讓極限、導數與積分有穩固基礎。可作為自學單元；請當成進入第 1 章前的暖身。

  1. Reading a function from its graph由圖形判讀函數
    Identify domain, range, and individual values directly off a plotted curve.直接由曲線判讀定義域、值域與個別函數值。
  2. New functions from old由舊函數造新函數
    Predict how shifts, stretches, and reflections move a graph before plotting them.在作圖前預測平移、伸縮與反射如何改變圖形。
  3. Families of functions函數族
    Vary one parameter and watch how the whole family of curves changes shape.改變單一參數，觀察整個曲線族如何改變形狀。
  4. Finding and graphing an inverse求反函數並作圖 planned規劃中
    Solve for an inverse and see it as the reflection of the original across the line y = x.解出反函數，並看出它是原函數對直線 y = x 的鏡射。
