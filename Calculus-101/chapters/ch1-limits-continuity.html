Chapter 1. Limits and Continuity第 1 章 · 極限與連續

The foundation of calculus. Work through the exercises below in order: each starts from a graph, asks you to read off a value, then has you justify it algebraically. Together they build up what a limit means, when it exists, and what makes a function continuous.微積分的基礎。請依序完成下列練習：每一題都從一張圖形開始，先請你判讀數值，再用代數方式說明理由。逐題累積後，你會理解極限的意義、何時存在，以及函數連續的條件。

  1. 1.1 Limits (An Intuitive Approach)1.1 極限（直觀方法）
    Read limits directly from graphs, compare one-sided limits, and distinguish limit values from function values.由圖形直接判讀極限，比較左右極限，並區分極限值與函數值。
  2. 1.2 Computing Limits1.2 計算極限
    Compute limits by direct substitution, algebraic simplification for 0/0 forms, and one-sided checks when needed.用直接代入、0/0 型化簡與必要時的單邊檢查來計算極限。
  3. Infinite limit near a vertical asymptote垂直漸近線附近的無窮極限 planned規劃中
    Read an infinite limit from both sides of a vertical asymptote and justify it algebraically.由垂直漸近線兩側判讀無窮極限，並用代數方式說明。
  4. The squeeze theorem夾擠定理 planned規劃中
    Trap an oscillating function between two parabolas to evaluate a limit the limit laws cannot reach.將振盪函數夾在兩條拋物線之間，求出極限定律無法直接處理的極限。
  5. Continuity of a piecewise function分段函數的連續性 planned規劃中
    Find the constant that makes a piecewise function continuous, using the three conditions for continuity.利用連續性的三個條件，求使分段函數連續的常數。
