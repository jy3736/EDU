Chapter 2. The Derivative第 2 章 · 導數

The derivative measures instantaneous rate of change. These exercises take you from the slope of a secant line, to the derivative as a limit, to the function that returns the slope at every point, and finally to the rules that let you compute it quickly.導數量度瞬時變化率。這些練習帶你從割線斜率出發，先以極限定義導數，再得到回傳每一點斜率的導函數，最後建立能快速計算導數的法則。

  1. Tangent line as a limit of secants以割線極限求切線 planned規劃中
    Watch secant slopes settle onto the tangent slope as the second point slides in.當第二點逐漸靠近時，觀察割線斜率收斂到切線斜率。
  2. Sketching f′ from the graph of f由 f 的圖形描繪 f′ planned規劃中
    Read where the slope is positive, negative, or zero and draw the derivative graph.判讀斜率為正、負或零之處，畫出導函數的圖形。
  3. The power and sum rules冪法則與和法則 planned規劃中
    Differentiate polynomials term by term and check against the limit definition.逐項對多項式求導，並與極限定義對照驗證。
  4. The product and quotient rules乘積與商法則 planned規劃中
    See why the derivative of a product is not the product of the derivatives.理解為何乘積的導數不是導數的乘積。
  5. The chain rule連鎖律 planned規劃中
    Peel a composite function layer by layer, multiplying the rates as you go.逐層拆解合成函數，沿途將各層變化率相乘。
