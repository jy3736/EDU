Chapter 3. Derivative in Graphing and Applications第 3 章 · 導數於繪圖與應用

Put the derivative to work. These exercises use the first and second derivatives to read a curve’s shape, locate its high and low points, and then solve real optimisation problems where the best choice is a maximum or a minimum.讓導數派上用場。這些練習運用一階與二階導數判讀曲線形狀、找出高低點，再求解最佳化問題，其中最佳選擇即為極大或極小值。

  1. Increase, decrease, and concavity遞增、遞減與凹性 planned規劃中
    Use the sign of f′ and f″ to describe a curve before you ever plot it.在作圖前，先用 f′ 與 f″ 的正負號描述曲線。
  2. Relative extrema and the derivative tests相對極值與導數判別法 planned規劃中
    Find critical points and classify each as a peak, a valley, or neither.求出臨界點，並判定各點為峰、為谷或皆非。
  3. Absolute maximum and minimum on an interval區間上的絕對極值 planned規劃中
    Compare critical values with the endpoints to find the true highest and lowest.比較臨界值與端點值，找出真正的最高與最低點。
  4. Applied optimisation應用最佳化 planned規劃中
    Turn a word problem into a function, then minimise cost or maximise area.將文字題化為函數，再求成本最小或面積最大。
  5. The Mean-Value Theorem均值定理 planned規劃中
    Find the point where the instantaneous rate equals the average rate.找出瞬時變化率等於平均變化率的點。
