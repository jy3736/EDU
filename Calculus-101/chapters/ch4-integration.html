Chapter 4. Integration第 4 章 · 積分

Integration adds up infinitely many small pieces. These exercises start from the area under a curve, build the definite and indefinite integral, and then reveal the Fundamental Theorem that ties integration back to the derivative.積分是把無窮多個小片段加總起來。這些練習從曲線下的面積出發，建立定積分與不定積分，再揭示將積分與導數連結起來的微積分基本定理。

  1. Area as a limit of rectangles以矩形極限求面積 planned規劃中
    Add up thinner and thinner rectangles and watch the total approach the exact area.將矩形切得越來越細並加總，觀察總和趨近精確面積。
  2. Indefinite integrals and antiderivatives不定積分與反導數 planned規劃中
    Reverse differentiation, and see why every answer carries a constant of integration.逆向操作微分，並理解為何每個答案都帶有一個積分常數。
  3. Integration by substitution變數代換積分 planned規劃中
    Run the chain rule backwards by choosing a substitution that simplifies the integrand.透過選取使被積函數變簡單的代換，將連鎖律反向運用。
  4. The definite integral定積分 planned規劃中
    Read a definite integral as signed area and estimate it from a graph.將定積分理解為帶號面積，並由圖形估計其值。
  5. The Fundamental Theorem of Calculus微積分基本定理 planned規劃中
    Use an antiderivative to evaluate a definite integral without summing rectangles.利用反導數求定積分，無須再加總矩形。
