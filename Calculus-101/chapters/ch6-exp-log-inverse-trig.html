Chapter 6. Exponential, Logarithmic, and Inverse Trig Functions第 6 章 · 指數、對數與反三角函數

The transcendental functions and their calculus. These exercises differentiate and integrate exponentials, logarithms, and inverse trigonometric functions, and use L’Hôpital’s rule to settle limits that come out as indeterminate forms.超越函數及其微積分。這些練習對指數、對數與反三角函數求導與積分，並運用羅必達法則處理結果為未定形的極限。

  1. Exponential and logarithmic functions指數與對數函數 planned規劃中
    See exponential growth and its inverse, the logarithm, as reflections across y = x.將指數成長與其反函數對數，看成對直線 y = x 的鏡射。
  2. Derivatives and integrals with ln x含 ln x 的導數與積分 planned規劃中
    Differentiate the logarithm and recover the integral of 1 / x.對對數函數求導，並還原 1 / x 的積分。
  3. Derivatives and integrals with e x含 e x 的導數與積分 planned規劃中
    Use the function that is its own derivative, in both differentiation and integration.運用「導數等於自身」的函數，於求導與積分中皆然。
  4. L’Hôpital’s rule羅必達法則 planned規劃中
    Resolve a 0 / 0 or ∞ / ∞ limit by differentiating top and bottom.對分子與分母分別求導，解決 0 / 0 或 ∞ / ∞ 型極限。
  5. Inverse trigonometric functions反三角函數 planned規劃中
    Differentiate arcsin and arctan, and recognise their integral forms.對 arcsin 與 arctan 求導，並辨認其對應的積分形式。
