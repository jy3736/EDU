A first-semester college calculus course for first-year engineering students, organised into six chapters. Each chapter is a sub-page holding several short learn-by-doing exercises: you read a graph or figure, fill in a reading table, work a hands-on task, then check a worked solution. The aim is to build the basic and fundamental ideas of each chapter step by step, not just to memorise rules. Use the buttons above to switch theme or language.本課程為工程學系一年級學生設計的第一學期大學微積分，分為六個章節。每一章為一個子頁面，內含數個短小的做中學練習：先判讀圖形、填寫判讀表、動手完成練習，再對照參考解答。目的是一步步建立各章的基本與核心觀念，而非僅記憶公式。可用上方按鈕切換主題或語言。

Chapters章節

  • 0 Before Calculus微積分之前 pp. 1–48 3 exercises3 個練習
    Precalculus review and diagnostic: the language of functions you will use all semester.微積分前的複習與診斷：本學期會反覆用到的函數語言。
    Exercises:練習： reading a function from its graph · shifting, stretching and reflecting curves · families of functions and their parameters · finding and graphing an inverse.由圖形判讀函數 · 平移、伸縮與反射曲線 · 函數族與其參數 · 求反函數並作圖。
  • 1 Limits and Continuity極限與連續 pp. 49–109 2 exercises2 個練習
    The foundation of calculus: what a limit means, when it exists, and what makes a function continuous.微積分的基礎：極限的意義、何時存在，以及函數連續的條件。
    Exercises:練習： limit at a removable discontinuity · one-sided limits at a jump · infinite limits and vertical asymptotes · the squeeze theorem · continuity of a piecewise function.可去間斷點的極限 · 跳躍點的單邊極限 · 無窮極限與垂直漸近線 · 夾擠定理 · 分段函數的連續性。
  • 2 The Derivative導數 pp. 110–186 building建置中
    From the slope of a secant line to the derivative function, plus the core rules for computing it.從割線斜率到導函數，並建立計算導數的核心法則。
    Exercises:練習： tangent line as a limit of secants · sketching f′ from the graph of f · power and sum rules · product and quotient rules · the chain rule.以割線極限求切線 · 由 f 的圖形描繪 f′ · 冪法則與和法則 · 乘積與商法則 · 連鎖律。
  • 3 Derivative in Graphing and Applications導數於繪圖與應用 pp. 187–264 building建置中
    Use the first and second derivatives to analyse curves and to solve optimisation problems.利用一階與二階導數分析曲線，並求解最佳化問題。
    Exercises:練習： increase, decrease and concavity · relative extrema and the derivative tests · absolute max and min on an interval · applied optimisation · the Mean-Value Theorem.遞增、遞減與凹性 · 相對極值與導數判別法 · 區間上的絕對極值 · 應用最佳化 · 均值定理。
  • 4 Integration積分 pp. 265–346 building建置中
    From the area problem to the definite and indefinite integral, tied together by the Fundamental Theorem.從面積問題到定積分與不定積分，並由微積分基本定理串連。
    Exercises:練習： area as a limit of rectangles · indefinite integrals and antiderivatives · integration by substitution · the definite integral · the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.以矩形極限求面積 · 不定積分與反導數 · 變數代換積分 · 定積分 · 微積分基本定理。
  • 6 Exponential, Logarithmic, and Inverse Trig Functions指數、對數與反三角函數 pp. 409–487 building建置中
    The transcendental functions and their calculus, including L’Hôpital’s rule for indeterminate forms.超越函數及其微積分，含處理未定形的羅必達法則。
    Exercises:練習： exponential and logarithmic functions · derivatives and integrals with ln x · derivatives and integrals with e x · L’Hôpital’s rule · inverse trigonometric functions.指數與對數函數 · 含 ln x 的導數與積分 · 含 e x 的導數與積分 · 羅必達法則 · 反三角函數。
