  1. Limit at a removable discontinuity可去間斷點的極限
    Evaluate a 0/0 limit by cancelling a common factor; the graph has a single hole.透過約去公因式求 0/0 型極限；圖形只有一個空心點。
  2. One-sided limits at a jump跳躍點的單邊極限
    Read the left and right limits of a piecewise function and decide if the two-sided limit exists.判讀分段函數的左右極限，並判斷雙邊極限是否存在。
  3. Infinite limit near a vertical asymptote垂直漸近線附近的無窮極限
    Read an infinite limit from both sides of a vertical asymptote and justify it algebraically.由垂直漸近線兩側判讀無窮極限，並用代數方式說明。
  4. The squeeze theorem夾擠定理
    Trap an oscillating function between two parabolas to evaluate a limit the limit laws cannot reach.將振盪函數夾在兩條拋物線之間，求出極限定律無法直接處理的極限。
  5. Continuity of a piecewise function分段函數的連續性
    Find the constant that makes a piecewise function continuous, using the three conditions for continuity.利用連續性的三個條件，求使分段函數連續的常數。
