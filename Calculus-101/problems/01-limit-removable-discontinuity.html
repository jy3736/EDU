1. Limit at a Removable Discontinuity1. 可去間斷點的極限

Problem題目

Consider the function \(f(x)=\dfrac{x^2-4}{x-2}\). The function is undefined at \(x=2\) because the denominator becomes zero there, so its graph has a single hole at that point, shown as an open circle below.考慮函數 \(f(x)=\dfrac{x^2-4}{x-2}\)。由於分母在 \(x=2\) 時為零，此函數在 \(x=2\) 沒有定義，因此圖形在該點有一個空心點（下圖以空心圓表示）。

A limit asks what value \(f(x)\) approaches as \(x\) gets close to 2, not what \(f(x)\) equals at 2. Evaluate極限探討的是當 \(x\) 趨近 2 時 \(f(x)\) 所趨近的值，而不是 \(f(x)\) 在 2 的值。試求

\[ \lim_{x\to 2}\frac{x^2-4}{x-2} \]

by reading the graph, then confirm it algebraically. As the dashed guides show, inputs near \(x=2\) push the outputs toward the height of the open circle.先由圖形判讀，再用代數驗證。如虛線所示，當輸入接近 \(x=2\) 時，輸出會趨近空心圓的高度。

What the graph shows圖形說明

Quantity量 Value值 Description說明
\(f(2)\) undefined未定義 The denominator \(x-2\) is zero, so the point is a hole.分母 \(x-2\) 為零，故此點為空心點。
Left limit左極限 4 As \(x\to 2^{-}\), outputs climb toward 4.當 \(x\to 2^{-}\)，輸出趨近 4。
Right limit右極限 4 As \(x\to 2^{+}\), outputs fall toward 4.當 \(x\to 2^{+}\)，輸出趨近 4。
Limit極限 4 Left and right agree, so the two-sided limit exists.左右極限相等，故雙邊極限存在。

Concepts you need解題所需概念

  • A limit describes approach, not arrival. \(\lim_{x\to a}f(x)\) is the value the outputs head toward as \(x\) nears \(a\). It can exist even when \(f(a)\) is undefined, because it never asks for the value at \(a\).極限描述「趨近」而非「到達」。\(\lim_{x\to a}f(x)\) 是當 \(x\) 接近 \(a\) 時輸出所趨向的值。由於它從不過問 \(a\) 當點的值，因此即使 \(f(a)\) 沒有定義，極限仍可能存在。
  • The form \(0/0\) is indeterminate. Substituting \(x=2\) gives \(\tfrac{0}{0}\), which is not a number. It is a signal that you must simplify first, not a final answer.\(0/0\) 是未定形。代入 \(x=2\) 得 \(\tfrac{0}{0}\)，它不是一個數，而是「必須先化簡」的訊號，並非最終答案。
  • Difference of squares. \(a^2-b^2=(a-b)(a+b)\), so here \(x^2-4=(x-2)(x+2)\). Spotting this factoring is what unlocks the cancellation.平方差公式。\(a^2-b^2=(a-b)(a+b)\)，故此處 \(x^2-4=(x-2)(x+2)\)。看出此分解正是能夠約分的關鍵。
  • Canceling is valid inside a limit. A limit only considers \(x\neq a\), where \(x-2\neq 0\). Dividing out the common factor \(x-2\) therefore does not change the limit, even though it would be illegal exactly at \(x=2\).在極限中約分是合法的。極限只考慮 \(x\neq a\) 的情形，此時 \(x-2\neq 0\)。因此約去公因式 \(x-2\) 不會改變極限值，儘管在 \(x=2\) 該點這麼做並不合法。
  • Removable discontinuity. A single hole where the limit exists but \(f(a)\) is missing. It is called "removable" because redefining \(f(a)\) to equal the limit would patch the hole and make \(f\) continuous.可去間斷點。單一空洞：極限存在，但 \(f(a)\) 缺失。之所以稱為「可去」，是因為只要把 \(f(a)\) 重新定義為該極限，即可補上空洞，使 \(f\) 連續。

Method: substitute \(x=a\); if you get \(\tfrac{0}{0}\), factor the numerator and denominator, cancel the common factor, then substitute into the simplified expression.解題步驟：先代入 \(x=a\)；若得 \(\tfrac{0}{0}\)，將分子與分母因式分解，約去公因式，再把 \(x=a\) 代入化簡後的式子。

Task練習

Evaluate the limit:求下列極限：

\[ \lim_{x\to 2}\frac{x^2-4}{x-2} \]

State the value, and justify it without substituting \(x=2\) directly.寫出極限值，並說明理由（不可直接代入 \(x=2\)）。

Hint:提示： The numerator factors as a difference of squares: \(x^2-4=(x-2)(x+2)\). For every \(x\neq 2\) the common factor \(x-2\) cancels, leaving a simpler expression whose value at \(x=2\) is the limit.分子可用平方差公式分解：\(x^2-4=(x-2)(x+2)\)。對所有 \(x\neq 2\)，公因式 \(x-2\) 可約去，剩下較簡單的式子，其在 \(x=2\) 的值即為極限。
Show solution顯示解答

Factor the numerator as a difference of squares and cancel the common factor:將分子以平方差分解，再約去公因式：

\[ \frac{x^2-4}{x-2}=\frac{(x-2)(x+2)}{x-2}=x+2 \qquad (x\neq 2) \]

The factor \(x-2\) cancels for every \(x\neq 2\), so the function agrees with the line \(y=x+2\) everywhere except at the hole. Therefore the limit is the height of that line at \(x=2\):因式 \(x-2\) 對所有 \(x\neq 2\) 都可約去，故此函數除了空心點外與直線 \(y=x+2\) 完全相同。因此極限等於該直線在 \(x=2\) 的高度：

\[ \lim_{x\to 2}\frac{x^2-4}{x-2}=\lim_{x\to 2}(x+2)=2+2=4 \]

The limit equals 4 even though \(f(2)\) itself is undefined.即使 \(f(2)\) 未定義，極限仍等於 4。
