2. One-Sided Limits at a Jump2. 跳躍點的單邊極限

Problem題目

Consider the piecewise function below. Different rules apply on the two sides of \(x=1\):考慮以下分段函數。在 \(x=1\) 兩側適用不同的式子：

\[ f(x)=\begin{cases} x+1, & x\lt 1 \\[2pt] x+4, & x\ge 1 \end{cases} \]

Its graph jumps at \(x=1\): the left piece rises to an open circle at \((1,2)\), while the right piece begins at a filled dot at \((1,5)\). A filled dot marks the actual function value; an open circle marks a value the graph approaches but does not reach on that side.其圖形在 \(x=1\) 處跳躍：左段上升到 \((1,2)\) 的空心圓，右段則從 \((1,5)\) 的實心點開始。實心點表示函數的實際值；空心點表示圖形在該側趨近、但未達到的值。

A one-sided limit asks what \(f(x)\) approaches from a single direction. Read both one-sided limits at \(x=1\) from the graph, then decide whether the two-sided limit \(\lim_{x\to 1} f(x)\) exists.單邊極限只探討 \(f(x)\) 從單一方向趨近的值。請由圖形判讀 \(x=1\) 的兩個單邊極限，再判斷雙邊極限 \(\lim_{x\to 1} f(x)\) 是否存在。

What the graph shows圖形說明

Task練習

Find the one-sided limits, then decide the two-sided limit:求兩個單邊極限，再判斷雙邊極限：

\[ \lim_{x\to 1^{-}} f(x)=\;?\qquad \lim_{x\to 1^{+}} f(x)=\;?\qquad \lim_{x\to 1} f(x)=\;? \]

where \(f(x)=x+1\) for \(x\lt 1\) and \(f(x)=x+4\) for \(x\ge 1\).其中 \(f(x)=x+1\)（\(x\lt 1\)），\(f(x)=x+4\)（\(x\ge 1\)）。

Hint:提示： For each one-sided limit, use the rule that applies on that side: the left limit uses \(x+1\), the right limit uses \(x+4\). A two-sided limit exists only when the two one-sided limits are equal.每個單邊極限請代入該側適用的式子：左極限用 \(x+1\)，右極限用 \(x+4\)。唯有當兩個單邊極限相等時，雙邊極限才存在。
Show solution顯示解答

From the left \((x\lt 1)\) the rule is \(f(x)=x+1\):左側 \((x\lt 1)\) 適用 \(f(x)=x+1\)：

\[ \lim_{x\to 1^{-}} f(x)=\lim_{x\to 1^{-}}(x+1)=1+1=2 \]

From the right \((x\ge 1)\) the rule is \(f(x)=x+4\):右側 \((x\ge 1)\) 適用 \(f(x)=x+4\)：

\[ \lim_{x\to 1^{+}} f(x)=\lim_{x\to 1^{+}}(x+4)=1+4=5 \]

The one-sided limits disagree, \(2\neq 5\). A two-sided limit exists only if both sides agree, so \(\lim_{x\to 1} f(x)\) does not exist.兩個單邊極限不相等，\(2\neq 5\)。雙邊極限唯有在兩側相等時才存在，故 \(\lim_{x\to 1} f(x)\) 不存在。

Note that \(f(1)=5\) is defined, yet the limit still fails to exist.注意 \(f(1)=5\) 有定義，但極限仍不存在。
