4. The Squeeze Theorem4. 夾擠定理

Problem題目

Evaluate the limit試求極限

\[ \lim_{x\to 0} x^2 \sin\frac{1}{x} \]

Direct substitution fails, and the product law does not help because \(\sin\frac{1}{x}\) oscillates faster and faster as \(x\to 0\), so \(\lim_{x\to 0}\sin\frac{1}{x}\) does not exist. Instead, trap the function between two simpler ones that share a common limit.直接代入無效，乘法定律也用不上，因為 \(\sin\frac{1}{x}\) 在 \(x\to 0\) 時振盪得越來越快，\(\lim_{x\to 0}\sin\frac{1}{x}\) 並不存在。改用兩個較簡單、且具有相同極限的函數，把它夾住。

The figure shows \(g(x)=x^2\sin\frac{1}{x}\) trapped between the envelopes \(y=x^2\) and \(y=-x^2\). Both envelopes pinch to 0 at the origin, dragging \(g\) with them.下圖顯示 \(g(x)=x^2\sin\frac{1}{x}\) 被夾在包絡線 \(y=x^2\) 與 \(y=-x^2\) 之間。兩條包絡線在原點收斂到 0，把 \(g\) 一起拉向 0。

Concepts you need解題所需概念

  • A bounded factor. For every input, \(-1\le\sin\theta\le 1\). So \(-1\le\sin\frac{1}{x}\le 1\) holds even though \(\lim_{x\to 0}\sin\frac{1}{x}\) does not exist (it oscillates).有界因子。對任何輸入，\(-1\le\sin\theta\le 1\)。因此即使 \(\lim_{x\to 0}\sin\frac{1}{x}\) 不存在（它在振盪），\(-1\le\sin\frac{1}{x}\le 1\) 仍恆成立。
  • Why the product law fails. The law \(\lim(fg)=\lim f\cdot\lim g\) requires each factor to have a limit. Since \(\sin\frac{1}{x}\) has none at \(0\), the law cannot be applied directly.為何不能用乘法定律。定律 \(\lim(fg)=\lim f\cdot\lim g\) 要求每個因子都有極限。但 \(\sin\frac{1}{x}\) 在 \(0\) 沒有極限，故不能直接套用。
  • The squeeze (sandwich) theorem. If \(g(x)\le f(x)\le h(x)\) near \(a\) and \(\lim_{x\to a}g=\lim_{x\to a}h=L\), then \(\lim_{x\to a}f=L\). The trapped function is forced to the shared limit.夾擠（三明治）定理。若在 \(a\) 附近 \(g(x)\le f(x)\le h(x)\)，且 \(\lim_{x\to a}g=\lim_{x\to a}h=L\)，則 \(\lim_{x\to a}f=L\)。被夾住的函數被迫趨向那個共同極限。
  • Multiplying by a nonnegative quantity preserves the inequality. Because \(x^2\ge 0\), multiplying \(-1\le\sin\frac{1}{x}\le 1\) by \(x^2\) keeps the \(\le\) signs pointing the same way.乘以非負量不改變不等式方向。由於 \(x^2\ge 0\)，將 \(-1\le\sin\frac{1}{x}\le 1\) 兩邊乘以 \(x^2\)，\(\le\) 的方向維持不變。
  • Bounded times vanishing goes to zero. A bounded factor multiplied by something heading to \(0\) (here \(x^2\to 0\)) is dragged to \(0\). The bounds \(\pm x^2\) make this precise.有界 × 趨零 ⇒ 趨零。有界因子乘上趨於 \(0\) 的量（此處 \(x^2\to 0\)）會被拉向 \(0\)。上下界 \(\pm x^2\) 把這件事說得精確。

Method: bound the oscillating factor between constants; multiply through by the vanishing factor to get \(g\le f\le h\); check that \(\lim g=\lim h\); conclude the same value by the squeeze theorem.解題步驟：先用常數把振盪因子夾住；兩邊乘以趨零的因子得到 \(g\le f\le h\)；確認 \(\lim g=\lim h\)；再由夾擠定理得到相同的極限值。

Task練習

Use the squeeze theorem to evaluate利用夾擠定理求

\[ \lim_{x\to 0} x^2 \sin\frac{1}{x} \]

State the bounding functions and explain why the bound is valid.寫出上下界函數，並說明此夾擠不等式為何成立。

Hint:提示： For every \(x\neq 0\), \(-1\le\sin\frac{1}{x}\le 1\). Multiply all three parts by \(x^2\), which is \(\ge 0\), so the direction of the inequalities is preserved.對所有 \(x\neq 0\)，\(-1\le\sin\frac{1}{x}\le 1\)。三邊同乘以 \(x^2\)（\(\ge 0\)），不等式方向不變。
Show solution顯示解答

The sine of anything stays within \([-1,1]\), so for every \(x\neq 0\)正弦值恆介於 \([-1,1]\)，故對所有 \(x\neq 0\)

\[ -1 \le \sin\frac{1}{x} \le 1 \]

Multiply through by \(x^2\), which is never negative, so the inequalities keep their direction:三邊同乘以恆非負的 \(x^2\)，不等式方向保持不變：

\[ -x^2 \le x^2\sin\frac{1}{x} \le x^2 \]

The two bounding functions have the same limit at 0:兩個界函數在 0 的極限相同：

\[ \lim_{x\to 0}\left(-x^2\right)=0=\lim_{x\to 0}x^2 \]

By the squeeze theorem, the trapped function shares that limit:由夾擠定理，被夾函數具有相同的極限：

\[ \lim_{x\to 0} x^2\sin\frac{1}{x}=0 \]
