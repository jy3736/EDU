5. Continuity of a Piecewise Function5. 分段函數的連續性

Problem題目

A function \(f\) is continuous at a point \(a\) when all three conditions hold: \(f(a)\) is defined, \(\lim_{x\to a}f(x)\) exists, and the two are equal. Consider the piecewise function函數 \(f\) 在點 \(a\) 連續須同時滿足三個條件：\(f(a)\) 有定義、\(\lim_{x\to a}f(x)\) 存在，且兩者相等。考慮分段函數

\[ f(x)=\begin{cases} x^2+c, & x\lt 1 \\[2pt] 2x, & x\ge 1 \end{cases} \]

where \(c\) is a constant. Find the value of \(c\) that makes \(f\) continuous at \(x=1\). The graph below is drawn for that value: the parabola piece joins the line piece with no break at \((1,2)\).其中 \(c\) 為常數。求使 \(f\) 在 \(x=1\) 連續的 \(c\) 值。下圖即以該值繪製：拋物線段與直線段在 \((1,2)\) 處銜接、沒有間斷。

Concepts you need解題所需概念

  • Continuity at a point: three conditions. \(f\) is continuous at \(a\) when (i) \(f(a)\) is defined, (ii) \(\lim_{x\to a}f(x)\) exists, and (iii) the two are equal: \(\lim_{x\to a}f(x)=f(a)\).點連續：三個條件。\(f\) 在 \(a\) 連續須滿足：(i) \(f(a)\) 有定義；(ii) \(\lim_{x\to a}f(x)\) 存在；(iii) 兩者相等：\(\lim_{x\to a}f(x)=f(a)\)。
  • Intuition. Continuous means no break: you can trace the graph through \(x=a\) without lifting your pen.直觀理解。連續代表沒有斷裂：可以一筆不離紙地描過 \(x=a\)。
  • At a piecewise boundary, condition (ii) is the work. The limit exists only when the one-sided limits match, \(\lim_{x\to a^{-}}f=\lim_{x\to a^{+}}f\), so this is where continuity usually succeeds or fails.分段邊界上，條件 (ii) 是重點。唯有左右極限相等 \(\lim_{x\to a^{-}}f=\lim_{x\to a^{+}}f\) 時極限才存在，因此連續與否通常就在此決定。
  • Polynomials are continuous everywhere. Each polynomial piece is automatically continuous on its own interval, so only the junction \(x=a\) needs checking.多項式處處連續。每段多項式在自己的區間上自動連續，故只需檢查接合點 \(x=a\)。
  • A free constant lets you enforce continuity. With an unknown like \(c\), set the boundary-matching condition equal and solve for the value that makes the limit exist and match \(f(a)\).用自由常數迫使連續。當式中有未知數（如 \(c\)），令邊界相等的條件成立並求解，得到使極限存在且等於 \(f(a)\) 的值。

Method: read \(f(a)\) from the piece that includes \(a\); compute both one-sided limits; set them equal to force the limit to exist; require that common value to equal \(f(a)\); solve for the constant.解題步驟：由包含 \(a\) 的那一段讀出 \(f(a)\)；計算兩個單邊極限；令其相等以迫使極限存在；要求該共同值等於 \(f(a)\)；解出常數。

Task練習

Find the constant \(c\) for which求使下列函數

\[ f(x)=\begin{cases} x^2+c, & x\lt 1 \\[2pt] 2x, & x\ge 1 \end{cases} \]

is continuous at \(x=1\), and verify all three conditions for continuity.在 \(x=1\) 連續的常數 \(c\)，並驗證連續性的三個條件。

Hint:提示： The right piece gives both \(f(1)\) and the right-hand limit. The left-hand limit uses \(x^2+c\). For \(\lim_{x\to 1}f(x)\) to exist, the two one-sided limits must be equal; setting them equal pins down \(c\).右段同時給出 \(f(1)\) 與右極限；左極限用 \(x^2+c\)。要使 \(\lim_{x\to 1}f(x)\) 存在，兩個單邊極限必須相等，令其相等即可解出 \(c\)。
Show solution顯示解答

Condition 1. Since \(1\ge 1\), use the second rule, so \(f(1)\) is defined:條件一. 因為 \(1\ge 1\)，用第二式，故 \(f(1)\) 有定義：

\[ f(1)=2(1)=2 \]

Condition 2. Compute the one-sided limits. From the right, \(f(x)=2x\); from the left, \(f(x)=x^2+c\):條件二. 計算單邊極限。右側 \(f(x)=2x\)，左側 \(f(x)=x^2+c\)：

\[ \lim_{x\to 1^{+}}2x=2, \qquad \lim_{x\to 1^{-}}(x^2+c)=1+c \]

The two-sided limit exists only when these agree, so雙邊極限唯有在兩者相等時才存在，故

\[ 1+c=2 \;\Longrightarrow\; c=1 \]

Condition 3. With \(c=1\), the limit equals the function value:條件三. 當 \(c=1\)，極限等於函數值：

\[ \lim_{x\to 1}f(x)=2=f(1) \]

All three conditions hold, so \(f\) is continuous at \(x=1\) precisely when \(c=1\).三個條件皆成立，故當且僅當 \(c=1\) 時 \(f\) 在 \(x=1\) 連續。
