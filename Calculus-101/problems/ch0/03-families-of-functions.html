0.3 Families of Functions0.3 函數族

1 · Concept and Fundamental Knowledge1 · 概念與基礎知識

Functions come in families: large groups that all share one algebraic form and differ only in a few constants. Change a constant and you slide from one member of the family to another. You already know several families: the power functions \(y=kx^n\), the polynomials (sums of power functions), the rational functions (one polynomial over another), and the sinusoids \(y=D+A\sin(Bx+C)\). This section reads the sinusoidal family off its graph, because each of its four constants controls exactly one feature you can point to.函數常以族的形式出現：一大群函數共用同一個代數形式，只在少數常數上不同。改變一個常數，就從族中的一個成員滑到另一個。你已認識數個函數族：冪函數 \(y=kx^n\)、多項式（冪函數之和）、有理函數（一個多項式除以另一個多項式），以及正弦型函數（弦波） \(y=D+A\sin(Bx+C)\)。本節直接由圖形判讀弦波族，因為它的四個常數各自控制一個你能指出的特徵。

Definition · A family of functions定義 · 函數族

A family of functions is a collection that all share the same form, written with one or more constants called parameters. Each choice of the parameters names one member. For the sinusoidal family函數族是一群共用同一形式的函數，其形式中含有一個或多個稱為參數的常數。每一組參數的選取對應族中的一個成員。對於弦波族

\[ y = D + A\sin\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr), \]

the four parameters \(D,\ A,\ B,\ h\) set the midline, the amplitude, the period, and the phase shift, one each.四個參數 \(D,\ A,\ B,\ h\) 分別設定中線、振幅、週期與相位移，各管一項。

Read each parameter straight off the picture using these four named features:利用以下四個具名特徵，可由圖形直接讀出每個參數：

Key terms · reading a sinusoid重要名詞 · 判讀弦波

  • Midline \(D\). The horizontal level the wave swings around, halfway between the crests and the troughs. It is the vertical shift, and \(D=\dfrac{\text{max}+\text{min}}{2}\).中線 \(D\)。波形上下擺動所環繞的水平高度，位於波峰與波谷的正中間。它就是垂直平移，且 \(D=\dfrac{\text{最大}+\text{最小}}{2}\)。
  • Amplitude \(|A|\). The distance from the midline up to a crest (the same as the distance down to a trough). So \(|A|=\dfrac{\text{max}-\text{min}}{2}\).振幅 \(|A|\)。由中線向上到波峰的距離（等於向下到波谷的距離）。故 \(|A|=\dfrac{\text{最大}-\text{最小}}{2}\)。
  • Period \(\dfrac{2\pi}{|B|}\). The horizontal length of one full cycle, for example the run from one crest to the next. The constant \(B\) sets how fast the wave oscillates.週期 \(\dfrac{2\pi}{|B|}\)。一個完整週期的水平長度，例如由一個波峰到下一個波峰的距離。常數 \(B\) 決定波形振盪的快慢。
  • Phase shift \(h\). The horizontal slide of the whole wave. In the form \(A\sin\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr)\) the graph moves \(h\) to the right; track it by watching where a crest lands.相位移 \(h\)。整個波形的水平平移。在 \(A\sin\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr)\) 的形式中，圖形向右移動 \(h\)；可由波峰落在何處來追蹤。

Rule · outside acts vertically, inside acts horizontally規則 · 外側管垂直、內側管水平

In \(y=D+A\sin\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr)\), the constants outside the sine act vertically: \(D\) raises the midline and \(A\) scales the amplitude. The constants inside the sine act horizontally: \(B\) scales the period \(\bigl(=\tfrac{2\pi}{|B|}\bigr)\) and \(h\) shifts the phase. As in Section 0.2, an inside change runs the opposite way to its sign, so \(\sin\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr)\) with \(h\gt 0\) moves the wave to the right.在 \(y=D+A\sin\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr)\) 中，正弦外側的常數作用於垂直方向：\(D\) 抬高中線、\(A\) 縮放振幅。正弦內側的常數作用於水平方向：\(B\) 縮放週期 \(\bigl(=\tfrac{2\pi}{|B|}\bigr)\)、\(h\) 平移相位。如同 0.2 節，內側的改變與其正負號相反，故 \(h\gt 0\) 時 \(\sin\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr)\) 把波形右移。

2 · Worked Examples2 · 範例（詳解）

All three figures use the same window \(0\le x\le 4\) and share the midline \(D=4\) (the grid line at \(y=4\)). Each example changes one parameter of the wave. Read the graph, then check it against the rule.三張圖都使用相同的視窗 \(0\le x\le 4\)，並共用中線 \(D=4\)（即 \(y=4\) 的格線）。每個範例改變波形的一個參數。請判讀圖形，再對照規則。

Example 1 · Amplitude and midline範例 1 · 振幅與中線

The wave below is \(s(x)=4+2\sin\!\bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{2}x\bigr)\). Read its midline, its amplitude, its period, and the value \(s(1)\).下方的波形是 \(s(x)=4+2\sin\!\bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{2}x\bigr)\)。請讀出它的中線、振幅、週期，以及函數值 \(s(1)\)。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
Midline中線 \(4\) The wave is symmetric about \(y=4\), halfway between the crest \(6\) and the trough \(2\): \(D=\tfrac{6+2}{2}=4\).波形對 \(y=4\) 對稱，位於波峰 \(6\) 與波谷 \(2\) 的正中間：\(D=\tfrac{6+2}{2}=4\)。
Amplitude振幅 \(2\) From the midline \(4\) up to the crest \(6\) is \(2\): \(|A|=\tfrac{6-2}{2}=2\).由中線 \(4\) 向上到波峰 \(6\) 為 \(2\)：\(|A|=\tfrac{6-2}{2}=2\)。
Period週期 \(4\) One full cycle runs from the up-crossing at \(x=0\) to the next at \(x=4\). Then \(B=\tfrac{2\pi}{4}=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\).一個完整週期由 \(x=0\) 的上行過中線點到 \(x=4\) 的下一個。於是 \(B=\tfrac{2\pi}{4}=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\)。
\(s(1)\) \(6\) At \(x=1\) the wave is at its crest; go up from \(x=1\) to the curve and across: the height is \(6\).在 \(x=1\) 波形達到波峰；由 \(x=1\) 上移到曲線再水平移過去：高度為 \(6\)。

Midline and amplitude come from one calculation: the midline is the average of the max and min, the amplitude is half their difference. Both are vertical (outside) features that the period and phase never touch.中線與振幅來自同一個計算：中線是最大與最小的平均，振幅是其差的一半。兩者都是垂直（外側）特徵，週期與相位都不會影響它們。

Example 2 · A shorter period範例 2 · 較短的週期

The next wave is \(t(x)=4+2\sin(\pi x)\). The midline and amplitude are the same as before, but the wave now oscillates faster. Read its period, the constant \(B\), and the value \(t(0.5)\).下一個波形是 \(t(x)=4+2\sin(\pi x)\)。中線與振幅和先前相同，但波形振盪得更快。請讀出它的週期、常數 \(B\)，以及函數值 \(t(0.5)\)。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
Amplitude振幅 \(2\) Unchanged: the crests still reach \(6\) and the troughs \(2\), so \(|A|=2\).不變：波峰仍到 \(6\)、波谷仍到 \(2\)，故 \(|A|=2\)。
Period週期 \(2\) One full cycle now fits in \(2\) units: the first crest is at \(x=0.5\), the next at \(x=2.5\), a gap of \(2\).一個完整週期現在只佔 \(2\) 個單位：第一個波峰在 \(x=0.5\)，下一個在 \(x=2.5\)，相距 \(2\)。
\(B\) \(\pi\) From period \(=\dfrac{2\pi}{B}=2\) we get \(B=\pi\), twice the \(\tfrac{\pi}{2}\) of Example 1.由週期 \(=\dfrac{2\pi}{B}=2\) 得 \(B=\pi\)，是範例 1 中 \(\tfrac{\pi}{2}\) 的兩倍。
\(t(0.5)\) \(6\) The first crest sits at \(x=0.5\); read straight up to the curve, height \(6\).第一個波峰在 \(x=0.5\)；直接上移到曲線，高度 \(6\)。

Notice · bigger B means a shorter period注意 · B 越大、週期越短

Doubling \(B\) from \(\tfrac{\pi}{2}\) to \(\pi\) halved the period, from \(4\) to \(2\). The period is \(\dfrac{2\pi}{|B|}\), so \(B\) and the period move in opposite directions. Do not confuse the period (how many \(x\)-units per cycle) with the frequency (how many cycles per unit), which is its reciprocal.把 \(B\) 由 \(\tfrac{\pi}{2}\) 加倍到 \(\pi\)，週期反而減半，由 \(4\) 變 \(2\)。週期是 \(\dfrac{2\pi}{|B|}\)，故 \(B\) 與週期方向相反。別把週期（每個週期佔多少 \(x\) 單位）與頻率（每單位有幾個週期，即其倒數）搞混。

Example 3 · A phase shift範例 3 · 相位移

This wave is \(u(x)=4+2\sin\!\bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{2}(x-1)\bigr)\). It has the same amplitude and period as Example 1, but the whole wave has slid sideways. Read its period, its phase shift, and the value \(u(2)\).這個波形是 \(u(x)=4+2\sin\!\bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{2}(x-1)\bigr)\)。它的振幅與週期和範例 1 相同，但整個波形已橫向滑動。請讀出它的週期、相位移，以及函數值 \(u(2)\)。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
Period週期 \(4\) One crest to the next is still \(4\) units apart, so \(B=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\), the same as Example 1.一個波峰到下一個仍相距 \(4\) 個單位，故 \(B=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\)，與範例 1 相同。
Phase shift相位移 \(1\) In Example 1 the crest was at \(x=1\); here it sits at \(x=2\). The wave slid right by \(1\), so \(h=1\).範例 1 的波峰在 \(x=1\)；這裡在 \(x=2\)。波形右移 \(1\)，故 \(h=1\)。
\(u(2)\) \(6\) The shifted crest is at \(x=2\); read up to the curve, height \(6\).移動後的波峰在 \(x=2\)；上移到曲線，高度 \(6\)。

A phase shift is an inside (horizontal) change. Writing the inside as \(B(x-h)\) makes the shift easy to read: the wave moves \(h\) to the right, here \(h=1\), exactly the opposite-sign rule from Section 0.2.相位移是內側（水平）改變。把內側寫成 \(B(x-h)\) 可讓平移容易讀出：波形右移 \(h\)，此處 \(h=1\)，正是 0.2 節相反符號規則的應用。

3 · Exercises3 · 練習

Exercise 1 · Amplitude and range練習 1 · 振幅與值域

Now it is your turn. The wave \(w\) below has midline \(y=4\) and runs over \(0\le x\le 4\). No values are marked; read them off the graph.換你試試。下方的波形 \(w\) 中線為 \(y=4\)，範圍為 \(0\le x\le 4\)。圖上未標數值；請自行判讀。

Using the graph of \(w\), answer all five parts:利用 \(w\) 的圖形，回答以下五個小題：

  1. State the amplitude of \(w\).寫出 \(w\) 的振幅。
  2. State the period of \(w\).寫出 \(w\) 的週期。
  3. State the range of \(w\) in interval notation.以區間記號寫出 \(w\) 的值域。
  4. Find \(w(2)\).求 \(w(2)\)。
  5. Write \(w\) in the form \(y=D+A\sin(Bx)\), giving \(D\), \(A\), and \(B\), and say at which \(x\) in \([0,4]\) the maximum occurs.將 \(w\) 寫成 \(y=D+A\sin(Bx)\) 的形式，給出 \(D\)、\(A\)、\(B\)，並說明在 \([0,4]\) 中最大值發生於哪個 \(x\)。
Hint:提示： Read the crest and the trough heights. The midline is their average, the amplitude is half their difference, and the range runs from the trough up to the crest. The period is the crest-to-crest distance.讀出波峰與波谷的高度。中線是兩者的平均，振幅是其差的一半，值域由波谷到波峰。週期是波峰到波峰的距離。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

(1) Amplitude. The crest reaches \(7\) and the trough \(1\), about the midline \(4\). The amplitude is half their difference:(1) 振幅。波峰到 \(7\)、波谷到 \(1\)，環繞中線 \(4\)。振幅為兩者差的一半：

\[ |A|=\frac{7-1}{2}=3. \]

(2) Period. One full cycle runs from the up-crossing at \(x=0\) to the next at \(x=4\), so:(2) 週期。一個完整週期由 \(x=0\) 的上行過中線點到 \(x=4\) 的下一個，故：

\[ \text{period}=4. \]

(3) Range. The outputs run from the trough \(1\) up to the crest \(7\):(3) 值域。輸出由波谷 \(1\) 到波峰 \(7\)：

\[ \text{range}(w)=[1,7]. \]

(4) \(w(2)\). At \(x=2\) the wave is back on the midline (an up-crossing on the way down), so:(4) \(w(2)\)。在 \(x=2\) 波形回到中線（下行過中線點），故：

\[ w(2)=4. \]

(5) Formula. The midline is \(D=4\), the amplitude is \(A=3\), and period \(4\) gives \(B=\tfrac{2\pi}{4}=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\). The crest is at \(x=1\):(5) 公式。中線 \(D=4\)、振幅 \(A=3\)，週期 \(4\) 給出 \(B=\tfrac{2\pi}{4}=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\)。波峰在 \(x=1\)：

\[ w(x)=4+3\sin\!\Bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{2}x\Bigr),\qquad \text{max at } x=1. \]

Sense check. The midline \(4\) is the average of crest \(7\) and trough \(1\), and the range \([1,7]\) is the midline plus or minus the amplitude \(3\). The reading \(w(2)=4\) lies on the midline, inside \([1,7]\). Everything is consistent.合理性檢查。中線 \(4\) 是波峰 \(7\) 與波谷 \(1\) 的平均，值域 \([1,7]\) 即中線加減振幅 \(3\)。讀值 \(w(2)=4\) 落在中線上，且在 \([1,7]\) 內。一切一致。

Exercise 2 · A phase shift練習 2 · 相位移

This wave \(v\) again has midline \(y=4\) and the same amplitude and period as Example 1, but it has been slid sideways. Compare it with the base wave \(4+2\sin\!\bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{2}x\bigr)\), whose first crest is at \(x=1\).這個波形 \(v\) 同樣以 \(y=4\) 為中線，振幅與週期和範例 1 相同，但已被橫向滑動。請與基底波形 \(4+2\sin\!\bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{2}x\bigr)\) 比較，後者的第一個波峰在 \(x=1\)。

Using the graph of \(v\), answer all five parts:利用 \(v\) 的圖形，回答以下五個小題：

  1. State the amplitude of \(v\).寫出 \(v\) 的振幅。
  2. State the period of \(v\).寫出 \(v\) 的週期。
  3. State the phase shift: how far, and in which direction, has the base wave moved?寫出相位移：基底波形移動了多遠、往哪個方向？
  4. Find \(v(3)\).求 \(v(3)\)。
  5. Write \(v\) in the form \(y=4+2\sin\!\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr)\), giving \(B\) and \(h\), and say at which \(x\) in \([0,4]\) the maximum occurs.將 \(v\) 寫成 \(y=4+2\sin\!\bigl(B(x-h)\bigr)\) 的形式，給出 \(B\) 與 \(h\)，並說明在 \([0,4]\) 中最大值發生於哪個 \(x\)。
Hint:提示： The amplitude and period are unchanged from Example 1. For the shift, find where a crest sits: the base wave crests at \(x=1\), so the rightward shift \(h\) is the gap from \(1\) to the new crest.振幅與週期和範例 1 相同。求平移時，找出波峰位置：基底波形在 \(x=1\) 達波峰，故向右的平移 \(h\) 就是 \(1\) 到新波峰之間的距離。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

(1) Amplitude. The crest reaches \(6\) and the trough \(2\) about the midline \(4\):(1) 振幅。波峰到 \(6\)、波谷到 \(2\)，環繞中線 \(4\)：

\[ |A|=\frac{6-2}{2}=2. \]

(2) Period. The crests sit one cycle apart at the same spacing as Example 1, so:(2) 週期。波峰間隔一個週期，與範例 1 相同，故：

\[ \text{period}=4,\qquad B=\tfrac{2\pi}{4}=\tfrac{\pi}{2}. \]

(3) Phase shift. The base wave crests at \(x=1\); here the crest is at \(x=3\). The wave has slid right by \(2\):(3) 相位移。基底波形在 \(x=1\) 達波峰；這裡波峰在 \(x=3\)。波形向右滑動 \(2\)：

\[ h=2 \text{ (to the right)}. \]

(4) \(v(3)\). The shifted crest is at \(x=3\), so:(4) \(v(3)\)。移動後的波峰在 \(x=3\)，故：

\[ v(3)=6. \]

(5) Formula. With \(B=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\) and \(h=2\):(5) 公式。取 \(B=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\)、\(h=2\)：

\[ v(x)=4+2\sin\!\Bigl(\tfrac{\pi}{2}(x-2)\Bigr),\qquad \text{max at } x=3. \]

Sense check. Amplitude \(2\) and midline \(4\) give the range \([2,6]\); the reading \(v(3)=6\) is the crest, the top of that range. The trough sits at \(x=1\), exactly \(2\) units left of the crest, confirming the shift. Everything is consistent.合理性檢查。振幅 \(2\) 與中線 \(4\) 給出值域 \([2,6]\)；讀值 \(v(3)=6\) 為波峰，即該值域的上界。波谷在 \(x=1\)，恰在波峰左方 \(2\) 個單位，印證了平移。一切一致。
