Chapter 1. Probability Models and Sets第 1 章 · 機率模型與集合

Weeks 1–2. We start by asking what a probability model is and why an engineer needs one, then build the set language (sample space, events, unions, intersections, complements) that the whole course rests on.第 1–2 週。先釐清機率模型是什麼、工程師為什麼需要它，接著建立整個課程會用到的集合語言（樣本空間、事件、聯集、交集、補集）。

  1. Probability models and the sample space機率模型與樣本空間
    List the outcomes of a random experiment and describe them as a sample space.列出隨機實驗的結果，並以樣本空間描述它們。
  2. Sets, events, and Venn diagrams集合、事件與范氏圖
    Read events off a Venn diagram and translate between words, sets, and pictures.由范氏圖判讀事件，並在文字、集合與圖形之間轉換。
  3. Set operations on events事件的集合運算
    Combine events with union, intersection, and complement, and use De Morgan laws.以聯集、交集、補集組合事件，並運用笛摩根定律。
