Chapter 2. Counting and Basic Probability第 2 章 · 計數與基本機率

Weeks 3–4. When outcomes are equally likely, probability is counting. We learn permutations and combinations, then state the probability axioms that every model must obey.第 3–4 週。當結果等可能時，機率問題通常會回到「怎麼數」。本章先學排列與組合，再整理所有機率模型都必須遵守的基本公理。

  1. Permutations and combinations排列與組合
    Count arrangements and selections, applied to passwords and communication codes.學會計算排序與選取方式，並套用到密碼與通訊編碼情境。
  2. The probability axioms機率公理
    The three axioms and the rules (complement, addition) that follow from them.掌握三條機率公理，以及由公理推出的補集律與加法律。
  3. Equally likely outcomes等可能結果
    Turn a count of favourable outcomes into a probability, with a simulation check.在等可能模型下，把有利結果數轉成機率，並用模擬檢查。
