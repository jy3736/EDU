Chapter 3. Conditional Probability and Independence第 3 章 · 條件機率與獨立性

Weeks 5–6. New information changes probabilities. We use probability trees and Bayes theorem to update beliefs (spam filtering, fault diagnosis), then define when two events are independent.第 5–6 週。新資訊會改變機率。我們以機率樹與貝氏定理更新判斷（垃圾郵件過濾、故障診斷），接著定義兩事件何時獨立。

  1. Conditional probability and trees條件機率與機率樹 planned規劃中
    Read a probability tree to find conditional and joint probabilities.判讀機率樹以求條件機率與聯合機率。
  2. Bayes theorem貝氏定理 planned規劃中
    Invert a conditional probability, applied to a spam filter and a fault detector.反轉條件機率，應用於垃圾郵件過濾器與故障偵測器。
  3. Independence of events事件的獨立性 planned規劃中
    Test whether two events are independent, and why it matters for reliability.檢驗兩事件是否獨立，以及它為何對可靠度重要。
