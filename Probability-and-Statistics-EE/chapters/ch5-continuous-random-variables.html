Chapter 5. Continuous Random Variables第 5 章 · 連續隨機變數

Weeks 10–12. When a quantity varies on a continuum, probability becomes area under a density curve. We read the PDF (uniform, exponential, Gaussian), tie noise to the Gaussian, and interpret the CDF.第 10–12 週。當一個量在連續區間上變化時，機率變成密度曲線下的面積。我們判讀機率密度函數（均勻、指數、高斯），將雜訊連結到高斯分布，並解讀累積分布函數。

  1. The PDF (uniform, exponential)機率密度函數（均勻、指數） planned規劃中
    Read probability as area under a density, for quantisation and waiting times.將機率視為密度下的面積，用於量化與等待時間。
  2. The Gaussian and measurement noise高斯分布與量測雜訊 planned規劃中
    Model measurement noise with the normal curve and use the 68-95-99.7 rule.以常態曲線建模量測雜訊，並運用 68-95-99.7 法則。
  3. The CDF and its interpretation累積分布函數及其解讀 planned規劃中
    Move between PDF and CDF and read off probabilities of intervals.在機率密度函數與累積分布函數之間轉換，並判讀區間機率。
