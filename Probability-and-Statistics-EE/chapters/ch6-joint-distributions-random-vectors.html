Chapter 6. Joint Distributions and Random Vectors第 6 章 · 聯合分布與隨機向量

Weeks 13–15. Real systems carry several random quantities at once. We read joint distributions as heatmaps, measure how a signal and its noise move together, and package several variables as a random vector.第 13–15 週。真實系統同時帶有多個隨機量。我們以熱圖判讀聯合分布，量測訊號與其雜訊如何一起變化，並將多個變數包裝成隨機向量。

  1. Joint distributions and heatmaps聯合分布與熱圖 planned規劃中
    Read a two-variable probability table as a heatmap and find the marginals.將雙變數機率表判讀為熱圖，並求邊際分布。
  2. Signal-noise relationships訊號與雜訊的關係 planned規劃中
    Measure covariance and correlation between a signal and the noise on it.量測訊號與其上雜訊之間的共變異數與相關係數。
  3. Random vectors in communication systems通訊系統中的隨機向量 planned規劃中
    Describe several random quantities together with a mean vector and its geometry.以平均向量及其幾何一起描述多個隨機量。
