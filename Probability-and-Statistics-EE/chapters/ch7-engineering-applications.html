Chapter 7. Engineering Applications and Project第 7 章 · 工程應用與專題

Weeks 16–17. We bring the whole course together on engineering problems: how long a system runs before failure, how a factory controls quality, and how a receiver decides between signal and noise, finishing with a small Monte Carlo project.第 16–17 週。我們在工程問題上整合整個課程：系統運轉多久才故障、工廠如何控管品質，以及接收器如何在訊號與雜訊之間做判斷，最後以一個小型蒙地卡羅專題收尾。

  1. Reliability and manufacturing quality可靠度與製造品質
    Use the exponential and binomial to model time-to-failure and defect rates.以指數與二項分布建模故障時間與不良率。
  2. Signal detection and Monte Carlo訊號偵測與蒙地卡羅
    Set a decision threshold against noise and estimate error rates by simulation.在雜訊下設定判斷門檻，並以模擬估計錯誤率。
  3. Mini project小型專題
    Apply the full predict-simulate-observe-theory cycle to an open engineering question.將完整的預測-模擬-觀察-理論循環應用於一個開放式工程問題。
