1.1 Probability Models and the Sample Space1.1 機率模型與樣本空間

1 · Concept and Fundamental Knowledge1 · 概念與基礎知識

Why an electronic engineer needs this. A receiver never knows in advance which bit pattern will arrive, how much noise a sample will carry, or whether a component will pass its test. These are random experiments: the outcome is uncertain before you run it. A probability model is the engineer’s way of writing down every possibility and how likely each one is, so that questions like “how often will this link drop a packet?” have a precise answer instead of a guess.電子工程師為什麼需要這個。接收器事先不會知道會收到哪個位元樣式、某個取樣帶有多少雜訊，或一個元件會不會通過測試。這些都是隨機實驗：執行之前，結果還不確定。機率模型就是工程師把所有可能結果與各自機率寫清楚的方法，讓「這條鏈路多久會掉一個封包？」這類問題有精確答案，而不是只靠猜測。

Definition · Experiment, outcome, sample space, event定義 · 實驗、結果、樣本空間、事件

A random experiment is any procedure whose result cannot be predicted with certainty. Each possible result is an outcome. The sample space \(S\) is the set of all outcomes. An event is any subset of \(S\): a collection of outcomes we care about. When an experiment is run, we say event \(A\) occurs if the outcome that happens is one of the outcomes in \(A\).隨機實驗是指結果無法事先完全確定的程序。每個可能結果稱為一個結果（outcome）。樣本空間 \(S\) 是所有結果所形成的集合。事件是 \(S\) 的任一子集合，也就是我們關心的一群結果。實驗執行後，如果實際發生的結果屬於 \(A\)，我們就說事件 \(A\) 發生。

The picture below shows the idea. The outer rectangle is the whole sample space \(S\) for rolling one die; each dot is one outcome. The blue circle is the event \(E=\{2,4,6\}\), “the result is even.” An event is literally a region you draw around the outcomes that make it true.下圖示範這個想法。外框矩形是擲一顆骰子的整個樣本空間 \(S\)，每個點代表一個結果。藍色圓圈是事件 \(E=\{2,4,6\}\)，也就是「結果為偶數」。事件可以看成把符合條件的結果圈起來的一塊區域。

Key terms · the language of a model重要名詞 · 模型的語言

  • Equally likely outcomes. If a model has \(N\) outcomes and symmetry makes no outcome special (a fair die, a fair coin, well-randomised data), each outcome has probability \(\tfrac{1}{N}\).等可能結果。若模型有 \(N\) 個結果，且對稱性使任一結果都不特別（公正骰子、公正硬幣、充分隨機化的資料），則每個結果的機率為 \(\tfrac{1}{N}\)。
  • Probability of an event. For equally likely outcomes, the probability of an event is a count: how many outcomes make it true, over the total.事件的機率。在等可能結果下，事件的機率就是一個計數：使其成立的結果數，除以總數。
  • The certain and the impossible event. The whole space \(S\) always occurs, so \(P(S)=1\); the empty set \(\varnothing\) never occurs, so \(P(\varnothing)=0\).必然事件與不可能事件。整個空間 \(S\) 一定會發生，所以 \(P(S)=1\)；空集合 \(\varnothing\) 不可能發生，所以 \(P(\varnothing)=0\)。

Simulate it first先模擬看看

Predict: if you roll a fair die many times, what fraction of rolls land on an even number? Write your guess, then run this and compare.預測：若擲一顆公正骰子很多次，落在偶數的比例會是多少？先寫下你的猜測，再執行並比較。

import numpy as np
rng = np.random.default_rng(0)

rolls = rng.integers(1, 7, size=100_000)   # 100k rolls of a fair die, values 1..6
is_even = (rolls % 2 == 0)                  # the event E = {2, 4, 6}
print(is_even.mean())                       # estimated P(E)

Observed: about \(0.4998\), right on the theoretical \(P(E)=\tfrac{3}{6}=\tfrac12\) below.觀察結果：約 \(0.4998\)，正好對應下方的理論值 \(P(E)=\tfrac{3}{6}=\tfrac12\)。

Rule · Counting probability for equally likely outcomes規則 · 等可能結果的計數機率

If every outcome in a finite sample space \(S\) is equally likely, then for any event \(A\),若有限樣本空間 \(S\) 中每個結果都等可能，則對任意事件 \(A\)，

\[ P(A)=\frac{\lvert A\rvert}{\lvert S\rvert}=\frac{\text{number of outcomes in } A}{\text{total number of outcomes}}. \]

Here \(\lvert A\rvert\) means the number of outcomes in \(A\). This is the bridge from counting to probability, and it is why the next chapter spends time on counting techniques.這裡 \(\lvert A\rvert\) 表示 \(A\) 中的結果個數。這就是從計數通往機率的橋樑，也是下一章要花時間談計數技巧的原因。

2 · Worked Examples2 · 範例（詳解）

Example 1 · One fair die範例 1 · 一顆公正骰子

Roll one fair die once. Write down the sample space, then find the probability of the event \(E=\{\,\text{the result is even}\,\}\) shown in the figure above.擲一顆公正骰子一次。寫出樣本空間，再求上圖中事件 \(E=\{\,\text{結果為偶數}\,\}\) 的機率。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
\(S\) \(\{1,2,3,4,5,6\}\) The six faces are all the possible results; list every dot in the rectangle.六個面就是所有可能結果；把矩形內每個點都列出來。
\(\lvert S\rvert\) \(6\) Count the outcomes in \(S\).數出 \(S\) 中的結果個數。
\(E\) \(\{2,4,6\}\) The outcomes inside the blue circle, the even faces.藍色圓圈內的結果，也就是偶數面。
\(P(E)\) \(\dfrac{3}{6}=\dfrac12\) Three even outcomes out of six equally likely outcomes.六個等可能結果中有三個偶數結果。

The whole method is in that last row: identify the outcomes in the event, count them, and divide by the size of the sample space. The simulation above landed on almost exactly \(\tfrac12\), confirming the count.整個方法都在最後一列：找出事件中的結果、數出個數、再除以樣本空間的大小。上方模擬幾乎落在 \(\tfrac12\)，印證了這個計數。

Example 2 · Two coin flips範例 2 · 擲兩枚硬幣

Flip a fair coin twice and record the pair of results in order. Now an outcome is a pair, not a single symbol. Find the probability of \(A=\{\,\text{at least one head}\,\}\). The new wrinkle: you must list ordered pairs to keep the outcomes equally likely.擲一枚公正硬幣兩次，依序記錄結果這一對。此時一個結果是一對，不是單一符號。求 \(A=\{\,\text{至少一個正面}\,\}\) 的機率。這題要注意：必須列出有序對，才能維持結果等可能。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
\(S\) \(\{HH,HT,TH,TT\}\) Each flip is H or T, and order matters, so there are \(2\times 2=4\) ordered pairs.每次擲出 H 或 T，而且順序不同就算不同結果，所以共有 \(2\times 2=4\) 個有序對。
\(A\) \(\{HH,HT,TH\}\) Every pair except \(TT\) has at least one head.除了 \(TT\) 之外，每一對都至少有一個正面。
\(P(A)\) \(\dfrac{3}{4}\) Three of the four equally likely pairs are in \(A\).四個等可能的對中，有三個屬於 \(A\)。

Notice · keep outcomes equally likely注意 · 維持結果等可能

A tempting wrong model lists the results as “0, 1, or 2 heads” and calls them three equally likely outcomes, giving \(P(A)=\tfrac23\). That is wrong: “1 head” happens two ways (\(HT\) and \(TH\)) but “2 heads” only one way. The counting rule needs equally likely outcomes, so list the four ordered pairs.一個常見但錯誤的模型，是把結果列成「0、1 或 2 個正面」，再把這三種當成等可能，得到 \(P(A)=\tfrac23\)。這是錯的：「1 個正面」有兩種方式（\(HT\) 與 \(TH\)），但「2 個正面」只有一種。計數規則需要等可能結果，所以要列出四個有序對。

Example 3 · A 3-bit register (engineering)範例 3 · 三位元暫存器（工程應用）

A 3-bit register holds a word \(b_2 b_1 b_0\). With well-randomised data every one of the \(2^3=8\) patterns is equally likely. In a one-hot scheme exactly one line should be high; find the probability of \(H=\{\,\text{exactly one bit is }1\,\}\). This is the same counting method, now over a larger sample space.一個三位元暫存器存放字組 \(b_2 b_1 b_0\)。在資料充分隨機化下，\(2^3=8\) 種樣式中的每一個都等可能。在 one-hot（單熱）編碼中恰好一條線為高電位；求 \(H=\{\,\text{恰好一個位元為 }1\,\}\) 的機率。這是同一套計數方法，只是樣本空間變大了。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
\(\lvert S\rvert\) \(8\) Each of the 3 bits is 0 or 1, so \(2^3=8\) patterns.3 個位元各為 0 或 1，所以有 \(2^3=8\) 種樣式。
\(H\) \(\{001,010,100\}\) The three patterns with a single 1, one per bit position.恰有一個 1 的三種樣式，每個位元位置各一。
\(P(H)\) \(\dfrac{3}{8}\) Three favourable patterns out of eight equally likely patterns.八種等可能樣式中有三種有利樣式。

Notice the method did not change at all when the experiment got more complicated: list \(S\), count it, count the event, divide. The only hard part in real problems is the counting itself, which is exactly what Chapter 2 develops.可以注意到，實驗變複雜時方法並沒有變：列出 \(S\)、數樣本空間、數事件、相除。真實問題中比較難的通常是「怎麼數」，這正是第 2 章要處理的內容。

3 · Exercises3 · 練習

Exercise 1 · A high roll練習 1 · 擲出大點數

Roll one fair die once. Consider the event \(B=\{\,\text{the result is greater than }4\,\}\). The sample space is drawn below with no event circle: deciding which outcomes belong to \(B\) is part of the task.擲一顆公正骰子一次。考慮事件 \(B=\{\,\text{結果大於 }4\,\}\)。下圖畫出樣本空間但未圈出事件：判斷哪些結果屬於 \(B\) 也是本題的一部分。

Answer all five parts:回答以下五個小題：

  1. List the sample space \(S\) and its size \(\lvert S\rvert\).列出樣本空間 \(S\) 及其大小 \(\lvert S\rvert\)。
  2. List the outcomes in \(B\).列出 \(B\) 中的結果。
  3. Find \(P(B)\).求 \(P(B)\)。
  4. Find the probability of the complementary event “the result is \(4\) or less.”求互補事件「結果為 \(4\) 或更小」的機率。
  5. Add your answers to parts 3 and 4. Why must they sum to \(1\)?把第 3 與第 4 小題的答案相加。為什麼兩者加起來一定是 \(1\)？
Hint:提示： “Greater than 4” does not include 4 itself. Every outcome is either in \(B\) or in its complement, never both, and together they fill \(S\).「大於 4」不包含 4 本身。每個結果不是落在 \(B\) 就是落在其互補事件，絕不會同時屬於兩者，而兩者合起來填滿 \(S\)。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

(1) Sample space. The die has six faces:(1) 樣本空間。骰子有六個面：

\[ S=\{1,2,3,4,5,6\},\qquad \lvert S\rvert=6. \]

(2) The event. “Greater than 4” means strictly above 4, so 5 and 6 only:(2) 事件。「大於 4」表示嚴格超過 4，所以只有 5 與 6：

\[ B=\{5,6\}. \]

(3) Probability. Two favourable outcomes out of six:(3) 機率。六個結果中有兩個有利：

\[ P(B)=\frac{2}{6}=\frac13. \]

(4) Complement. “\(4\) or less” is \(\{1,2,3,4\}\), four outcomes:(4) 互補事件。「\(4\) 或更小」為 \(\{1,2,3,4\}\)，共四個結果：

\[ P(\{1,2,3,4\})=\frac{4}{6}=\frac23. \]

(5) They sum to 1. \(\tfrac13+\tfrac23=1\). Every roll lands in exactly one of the two events, and together they are all of \(S\); since \(P(S)=1\), the two probabilities must add to \(1\).(5) 兩者加起來是 1。\(\tfrac13+\tfrac23=1\)。每次擲骰都會剛好落在兩事件之一，而且兩者合起來就是整個 \(S\)；因為 \(P(S)=1\)，兩個機率加起來一定是 \(1\)。

Sense check. \(P(B)=\tfrac13\) lies between 0 and 1, and \(B\) has fewer outcomes than its complement, so a probability below \(\tfrac12\) is reasonable.合理性檢查。\(P(B)=\tfrac13\) 介於 0 與 1 之間，且 \(B\) 的結果數少於其互補事件，因此機率小於 \(\tfrac12\) 是合理的。

Exercise 2 · Exactly one head練習 2 · 恰好一個正面

Flip a fair coin twice, recording the ordered pair. Consider \(C=\{\,\text{exactly one head}\,\}\). The four outcomes are drawn below with no event marked.擲一枚公正硬幣兩次，記錄有序對。考慮 \(C=\{\,\text{恰好一個正面}\,\}\)。下圖畫出四個結果，未標示事件。

Answer all five parts:回答以下五個小題：

  1. List the sample space \(S\) and its size.列出樣本空間 \(S\) 及其大小。
  2. List the outcomes in \(C\).列出 \(C\) 中的結果。
  3. Find \(P(C)\).求 \(P(C)\)。
  4. Find \(P(\{\,\text{two heads}\,\})\) and \(P(\{\,\text{no heads}\,\})\).求 \(P(\{\,\text{兩個正面}\,\})\) 與 \(P(\{\,\text{沒有正面}\,\})\)。
  5. A classmate claims “0, 1, or 2 heads, so \(P(C)=\tfrac13\).” Explain in one or two sentences why this is wrong.一位同學主張「0、1 或 2 個正面，所以 \(P(C)=\tfrac13\)」。用一兩句話說明這為什麼錯。
Hint:提示： Keep the outcomes as ordered pairs so they are equally likely. “Exactly one head” can happen in two orders.把結果保持為有序對，才會等可能。「恰好一個正面」可以有兩種順序發生。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

(1) Sample space. Two flips, order kept:(1) 樣本空間。擲兩次，保留順序：

\[ S=\{HH,HT,TH,TT\},\qquad \lvert S\rvert=4. \]

(2) The event. Exactly one head occurs as \(HT\) or \(TH\):(2) 事件。恰好一個正面為 \(HT\) 或 \(TH\)：

\[ C=\{HT,TH\}. \]

(3) Probability. Two of four equally likely pairs:(3) 機率。四個等可能對中有兩個：

\[ P(C)=\frac{2}{4}=\frac12. \]

(4) The other two events. Two heads is \(\{HH\}\); no heads is \(\{TT\}\):(4) 另外兩個事件。兩個正面為 \(\{HH\}\)；沒有正面為 \(\{TT\}\)：

\[ P(\{HH\})=\frac14,\qquad P(\{TT\})=\frac14. \]

(5) Why \(\tfrac13\) is wrong. The three counts “0, 1, 2 heads” are not equally likely: one head happens two ways (\(HT,TH\)) while two heads happens only one way. The counting rule \(P=\lvert A\rvert/\lvert S\rvert\) requires equally likely outcomes, so we must use the four ordered pairs, giving \(P(C)=\tfrac12\).(5) 為什麼 \(\tfrac13\) 是錯的。「0、1、2 個正面」這三個計數不是等可能：一個正面有兩種方式（\(HT,TH\)），但兩個正面只有一種。計數規則 \(P=\lvert A\rvert/\lvert S\rvert\) 需要等可能結果，所以必須使用四個有序對，得到 \(P(C)=\tfrac12\)。

Sense check. The four probabilities \(\tfrac14+\tfrac12+\tfrac14=1\) cover “no heads,” “one head,” and “two heads,” which together are all of \(S\). Good.合理性檢查。四個機率 \(\tfrac14+\tfrac12+\tfrac14=1\) 涵蓋「沒有正面」「一個正面」「兩個正面」，三者合起來就是整個 \(S\)。正確。
