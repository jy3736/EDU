1.2 Sets, Events, and Venn Diagrams1.2 集合、事件與范氏圖

1 · Concept and Fundamental Knowledge1 · 概念與基礎知識

Why an electronic engineer needs this. Real systems rarely have one thing going wrong at a time. A unit might overheat, or see a voltage spike, or both at once. To reason about “A and B,” “A but not B,” or “neither,” you need a picture that keeps the overlaps straight. The Venn diagram is that picture: it draws each event as a region inside the sample space and shows exactly how the events share outcomes.電子工程師為什麼需要這個。真實系統很少一次只出一個問題。一個單元可能過熱、遇到電壓突波，也可能兩者同時發生。要推理「A 且 B」「A 但非 B」或「兩者皆非」，需要一張能把重疊關係看清楚的圖。范氏圖就是這張圖：它把每個事件畫成樣本空間內的一塊區域，清楚顯示事件之間如何共用結果。

Definition · Venn diagram and the four basic regions定義 · 范氏圖與四個基本區域

A Venn diagram draws the sample space \(S\) as a rectangle and each event as a circle inside it. Two events \(A\) and \(B\) cut the rectangle into four regions: only \(A\), only \(B\), the overlap (in both), and neither (outside both). Because every outcome lands in exactly one region, the four region probabilities always add up to \(1\).范氏圖把樣本空間 \(S\) 畫成矩形，並把每個事件畫成矩形內的一個圓。兩個事件 \(A\) 與 \(B\) 會把矩形切成四個區域：僅 \(A\)、僅 \(B\)、重疊（同時屬於兩者）與兩者皆非（兩圓之外）。因為每個結果剛好落在一個區域，四個區域的機率加起來一定是 \(1\)。

The diagram below names the four regions. Read it slowly: the only place that is “in \(A\) and in \(B\)” is the lens-shaped overlap in the middle.下圖標出四個區域。慢慢讀：唯一「既屬 \(A\) 又屬 \(B\)」的地方，是中間透鏡形的重疊區。

Key terms · relationships between events重要名詞 · 事件之間的關係

  • Subset \(A\subseteq B\). Every outcome of \(A\) is also in \(B\); the circle for \(A\) sits entirely inside \(B\). Then \(A\) occurring forces \(B\) to occur.子集合 \(A\subseteq B\)。\(A\) 的每個結果都屬於 \(B\)；\(A\) 的圓完全落在 \(B\) 裡面。這時只要 \(A\) 發生，就能保證 \(B\) 也發生。
  • Mutually exclusive (disjoint). \(A\) and \(B\) share no outcome; their circles do not overlap. They cannot both happen, so the overlap region is empty.互斥（不相交）。\(A\) 與 \(B\) 沒有共同結果；兩圓不重疊。兩者不能同時發生，所以重疊區為空。
  • Complement \(\overline{A}\). Everything in \(S\) that is not in \(A\): the whole region outside the \(A\) circle.補集 \(\overline{A}\)。\(S\) 中所有不屬於 \(A\) 的部分：即 \(A\) 圓之外的整塊區域。

Simulate it first先模擬看看

Predict: a population is split into the four regions with probabilities only-\(A\) \(0.30\), both \(0.10\), only-\(B\) \(0.20\), neither \(0.40\). If you sample many individuals, what fraction land in \(A\) (either region that touches the \(A\) circle)? Guess, then run.預測：某母體依四個區域的機率劃分：僅 \(A\) 為 \(0.30\)、兩者為 \(0.10\)、僅 \(B\) 為 \(0.20\)、皆非為 \(0.40\)。若抽樣許多個體，落在 \(A\)（任何位在 \(A\) 圓內的區域）的比例是多少？先猜，再執行。

import numpy as np
rng = np.random.default_rng(0)

regions = ["onlyA", "both", "onlyB", "neither"]
probs   = [0.30,    0.10,   0.20,    0.40]
draws = rng.choice(regions, size=200_000, p=probs)

in_A = np.isin(draws, ["onlyA", "both"])    # A = only-A together with the overlap
print(in_A.mean())                           # estimated P(A)

Observed: about \(0.400\), matching \(P(A)=0.30+0.10=0.40\). The overlap counts toward \(A\), which is the whole point of the diagram.觀察結果：約 \(0.400\)，對應 \(P(A)=0.30+0.10=0.40\)。重疊區要算進 \(A\)，這正是范氏圖的重點。

Rule · an event is the sum of its regions規則 · 事件等於其各區域之和

On a Venn diagram, the probability of an event is the sum of the region probabilities the event covers. Because the \(A\) circle covers both “only \(A\)” and the overlap,在范氏圖上，事件的機率是該事件所涵蓋各區域機率之和。由於 \(A\) 圓涵蓋「僅 \(A\)」與重疊區，

\[ P(A)=P(\text{only }A)+P(A\cap B). \]

The same idea gives \(P(\overline{A})=1-P(A)\), since the regions inside and outside the \(A\) circle together fill \(S\).同理可得 \(P(\overline{A})=1-P(A)\)，因為 \(A\) 圓內外的區域合起來填滿 \(S\)。

2 · Worked Examples2 · 範例（詳解）

Example 1 · Reading a two-event Venn範例 1 · 判讀雙事件范氏圖

The Venn diagram below gives the probability sitting in each of the four regions. Read \(P(A)\), \(P(B)\), \(P(A\cap B)\), and the probability of neither event.下方范氏圖給出四個區域各自的機率。請讀出 \(P(A)\)、\(P(B)\)、\(P(A\cap B)\)，以及兩者皆非的機率。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
\(P(A\cap B)\) \(0.10\) The number in the central overlap lens.中央重疊透鏡內的數字。
\(P(A)\) \(0.40\) Add the two regions the \(A\) circle covers: \(0.30+0.10\).把 \(A\) 圓涵蓋的兩區相加：\(0.30+0.10\)。
\(P(B)\) \(0.30\) Add the two regions the \(B\) circle covers: \(0.20+0.10\).把 \(B\) 圓涵蓋的兩區相加：\(0.20+0.10\)。
\(P(\text{neither})\) \(0.40\) The region outside both circles.兩圓之外的區域。

The key idea is that the overlap probability is shared: it is counted once inside \(P(A)\) and once inside \(P(B)\). As a check, all four regions sum to \(0.30+0.10+0.20+0.40=1\), as they must.關鍵在於重疊機率是共用的：它在 \(P(A)\) 與 \(P(B)\) 中各算一次。驗算時，四區加總為 \(0.30+0.10+0.20+0.40=1\)，符合樣本空間總機率為 \(1\) 的要求。

Example 2 · Mutually exclusive events範例 2 · 互斥事件

A single packet is classified by its result: \(A=\{\text{delivered}\}\) and \(B=\{\text{dropped}\}\). These cannot both happen for one packet, so the circles do not overlap. The new wrinkle is reading a diagram where the overlap is empty.一個封包依結果分類：\(A=\{\text{送達}\}\) 與 \(B=\{\text{丟棄}\}\)。對單一封包來說，這兩者不能同時發生，所以兩圓不重疊。這題的新重點，是判讀重疊區為空的圖。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
\(P(A)\) \(0.85\) The \(A\) circle stands alone, so its single region is all of \(P(A)\).\(A\) 圓單獨存在，其唯一區域即為整個 \(P(A)\)。
\(P(A\cap B)\) \(0\) There is no overlap, so the events share no outcome.沒有重疊，所以兩事件沒有共同結果。
\(P(A\cup B)\) \(0.95\) With no shared part, just add: \(0.85+0.10\).沒有共用部分時直接相加：\(0.85+0.10\)。

Notice · mutually exclusive is not the same as covering everything注意 · 互斥不等於涵蓋全部

The two circles do not overlap, but they also do not fill \(S\): the \(0.05\) outside is “neither delivered nor dropped” (for instance, still in transit). Mutually exclusive only means the overlap is empty; it does not force the probabilities to add to \(1\).兩圓不重疊，但也未填滿 \(S\)：外側的 \(0.05\) 是「既未送達也未丟棄」（例如仍在傳輸中）。互斥僅表示重疊為空；它並不強迫機率之和為 \(1\)。

Example 3 · Two fault types from test data (engineering)範例 3 · 由測試資料看兩種故障（工程應用）

Out of \(200\) units on a test bench, \(30\) overheat only, \(10\) both overheat and show a voltage spike, \(20\) show a spike only, and \(140\) show neither. Let \(A=\{\text{overheats}\}\) and \(B=\{\text{voltage spike}\}\). Find the probabilities. The new element is converting counts into probabilities by dividing by \(200\).測試台上 \(200\) 個單元中，\(30\) 個僅過熱、\(10\) 個同時過熱且有電壓突波、\(20\) 個僅有突波、\(140\) 個兩者皆無。令 \(A=\{\text{過熱}\}\)、\(B=\{\text{電壓突波}\}\)。求各機率。這題的新重點，是把計數除以 \(200\)，轉成機率。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量 Value值 How you read it判讀方式
\(P(A)\) \(\dfrac{40}{200}=0.20\) \(A\) covers \(30+10=40\) units, over \(200\).\(A\) 涵蓋 \(30+10=40\) 個單元，除以 \(200\)。
\(P(B)\) \(\dfrac{30}{200}=0.15\) \(B\) covers \(20+10=30\) units, over \(200\).\(B\) 涵蓋 \(20+10=30\) 個單元，除以 \(200\)。
\(P(A\cap B)\) \(\dfrac{10}{200}=0.05\) The \(10\) units in the overlap, over \(200\).重疊區的 \(10\) 個單元，除以 \(200\)。
\(P(\overline{A})\) \(0.80\) Complement: \(1-P(A)=1-0.20\).補集：\(1-P(A)=1-0.20\)。

Counts and probabilities carry the same information once you divide by the total. The four counts \(30+10+20+140=200\) recover the whole batch, the count version of “the regions sum to \(1\).”計數除以總數之後，就會得到同一份資訊的機率版本。四個計數 \(30+10+20+140=200\) 還原整批，正是「各區域機率加起來為 \(1\)」的計數版本。

3 · Exercises3 · 練習

Exercise 1 · Reading region probabilities練習 1 · 判讀區域機率

The Venn diagram gives the probability in each region for two events \(A\) and \(B\). Use it to answer the parts below.下方范氏圖給出兩事件 \(A\) 與 \(B\) 各區域的機率。據此回答以下小題。

Answer all five parts:回答以下五個小題：

  1. Find \(P(A\cap B)\).求 \(P(A\cap B)\)。
  2. Find \(P(A)\).求 \(P(A)\)。
  3. Find \(P(B)\).求 \(P(B)\)。
  4. Find \(P(\overline{B})\), the probability that \(B\) does not occur.求 \(P(\overline{B})\)，即 \(B\) 不發生的機率。
  5. Verify that the four region probabilities sum to \(1\).驗證四個區域機率之和為 \(1\)。
Hint:提示： A circle’s probability is the sum of the regions it covers. For \(P(\overline{B})\), use \(1-P(B)\) or add every region the \(B\) circle does not cover.一個圓的機率是其涵蓋各區域之和。求 \(P(\overline{B})\) 時，用 \(1-P(B)\)，或把 \(B\) 圓未涵蓋的所有區域相加。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

(1) Overlap. Read the central lens directly:(1) 重疊。直接讀中央透鏡：

\[ P(A\cap B)=0.15. \]

(2) \(P(A)\). The \(A\) circle covers only-\(A\) and the overlap:(2) \(P(A)\)。\(A\) 圓涵蓋「僅 \(A\)」與重疊：

\[ P(A)=0.25+0.15=0.40. \]

(3) \(P(B)\). The \(B\) circle covers only-\(B\) and the overlap:(3) \(P(B)\)。\(B\) 圓涵蓋「僅 \(B\)」與重疊：

\[ P(B)=0.35+0.15=0.50. \]

(4) Complement of \(B\). Everything outside the \(B\) circle, namely only-\(A\) plus neither, or simply \(1-P(B)\):(4) \(B\) 的補集。\(B\) 圓之外的一切，即「僅 \(A\)」加「皆非」，或直接 \(1-P(B)\)：

\[ P(\overline{B})=0.25+0.25=0.50=1-0.50. \]

(5) Sum check.(5) 總和驗證。

\[ 0.25+0.15+0.35+0.25=1.00. \]

Sense check. Every probability lies in \([0,1]\); \(P(A\cap B)=0.15\) is no larger than either \(P(A)=0.40\) or \(P(B)=0.50\), as an overlap must be. Consistent.合理性檢查。每個機率都落在 \([0,1]\)；\(P(A\cap B)=0.15\) 不大於 \(P(A)=0.40\) 或 \(P(B)=0.50\)，符合重疊的性質。一致。

Exercise 2 · Filling in the regions練習 2 · 補齊各區域

A batch of \(100\) boards is tested for two defects. The diagram shows only what is known directly: \(12\) boards have both defects, the \(A\) circle holds \(30\) boards in total, and the \(B\) circle holds \(25\) boards in total. The single-defect regions are left blank for you to work out.一批 \(100\) 片電路板檢測兩種瑕疵。圖中只標出直接已知的部分：\(12\) 片同時有兩種瑕疵、\(A\) 圓共有 \(30\) 片、\(B\) 圓共有 \(25\) 片。單一瑕疵的區域留白，由你求出。

Answer all five parts:回答以下五個小題：

  1. How many boards have defect \(A\) only?有僅 \(A\) 瑕疵的板子有幾片？
  2. How many boards have defect \(B\) only?有僅 \(B\) 瑕疵的板子有幾片？
  3. How many boards have neither defect?兩種瑕疵皆無的板子有幾片？
  4. Find \(P(A\cap B)\) and \(P(\overline{A})\).求 \(P(A\cap B)\) 與 \(P(\overline{A})\)。
  5. Are \(A\) and \(B\) mutually exclusive? Justify from the diagram.\(A\) 與 \(B\) 互斥嗎？請依圖說明。
Hint:提示： “Only \(A\)” is the \(A\) total minus the overlap. Once all four regions are filled, they must add to \(100\).「僅 \(A\)」是 \(A\) 的總數減去重疊。四個區域填齊後，總和必為 \(100\)。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

(1) Only \(A\). The \(A\) circle holds \(30\) boards and \(12\) of them are in the overlap, so the rest are \(A\) only:(1) 僅 \(A\)。\(A\) 圓有 \(30\) 片，其中 \(12\) 片在重疊區，其餘為僅 \(A\)：

\[ 30-12=18. \]

(2) Only \(B\). Likewise from the \(B\) total of \(25\):(2) 僅 \(B\)。同理，由 \(B\) 的總數 \(25\)：

\[ 25-12=13. \]

(3) Neither. The four regions fill the batch of \(100\):(3) 皆無。四個區域填滿這批 \(100\) 片：

\[ 100-(18+12+13)=100-43=57. \]

(4) Two probabilities. Divide counts by \(100\):(4) 兩個機率。把計數除以 \(100\)：

\[ P(A\cap B)=\frac{12}{100}=0.12,\qquad P(\overline{A})=1-\frac{30}{100}=0.70. \]

(5) Mutually exclusive? No. The overlap holds \(12\) boards, so \(A\) and \(B\) do occur together; mutually exclusive would require an empty overlap.(5) 互斥嗎？否。重疊區有 \(12\) 片，所以 \(A\) 與 \(B\) 確實會同時發生；互斥必須是重疊區為空。

Sense check. All four regions \(18+12+13+57=100\) recover the batch, and each “only” count is below its circle total, exactly as carving out the overlap demands.合理性檢查。四區 \(18+12+13+57=100\) 還原整批，且每個「僅」的計數都小於其圓總數，正符合扣除重疊的要求。
