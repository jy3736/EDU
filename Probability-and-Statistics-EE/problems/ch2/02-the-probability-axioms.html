2.2 The Probability Axioms2.2 機率公理

1 · Concept and Fundamental Knowledge1 · 概念與基礎知識

Why an electronic engineer needs this. A reliability spreadsheet, a channel-error model, or a simulation histogram can look numerical but still be invalid. The axioms are the quick consistency checks that keep probability models honest.電子工程師為什麼需要這個。可靠度試算表、通道錯誤模型或模擬直方圖看起來都有數字，但不代表一定合理。機率公理就是快速檢查模型是否站得住腳的基本規則。

Definition · probability measure定義 · 機率測度

A probability measure assigns a number \(P(A)\) to each event \(A\) in the sample space. The assignment is valid only if it obeys the probability axioms.機率測度會為樣本空間中的每個事件 \(A\) 指定一個數字 \(P(A)\)。只有這些數字符合機率公理時，這個模型才算有效。

Key terms · valid model checks重要名詞 · 有效模型檢查

  • Nonnegative. No event can have probability below 0.非負。任何事件的機率都不可小於 0。
  • Normalized. The whole sample space has probability 1.正規化。整個樣本空間的機率為 1。
  • Additive for disjoint events. If two events do not overlap, the probability of their union is the sum of their probabilities.互斥可加。若兩事件不重疊，其聯集機率等於兩者機率相加。

Simulate it first先模擬看看

Predict: if four disjoint regions have probabilities 0.20, 0.30, 0.10, and 0.40, what fraction should land in the first two regions together?預測：若四個互斥區域的機率為 0.20、0.30、0.10、0.40，落在前兩個區域合併事件的比例應該是多少？

import numpy as np
rng = np.random.default_rng(0)

regions = rng.choice(["R1", "R2", "R3", "R4"], size=200_000,
                     p=[0.20, 0.30, 0.10, 0.40])
event = np.isin(regions, ["R1", "R2"])
print(event.mean())

Observed: about \(0.50\), matching \(0.20+0.30\) because the regions are disjoint.觀察結果：約 \(0.50\)，符合 \(0.20+0.30\)，因為這些區域互斥。

Rule · the three axioms規則 · 三條公理

\[ P(A)\ge 0,\qquad P(S)=1,\qquad A\cap B=\varnothing \Rightarrow P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B). \]

From these follow the complement rule \(P(\overline{A})=1-P(A)\) and the general addition law \(P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(A\cap B)\).從這三條公理可以推出補集律 \(P(\overline{A})=1-P(A)\)，以及一般加法律 \(P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(A\cap B)\)。

Example 1 · Is this PMF valid?範例 1 · 這個 PMF 有效嗎？

A packet error counter can be 0, 1, or 2 errors, with probabilities 0.70, 0.20, and 0.10. Is this a valid model?某封包錯誤計數可能是 0、1、2 個錯誤，其機率為 0.70、0.20、0.10。這是有效模型嗎？

Nonnegative非負yes是All three values are at least 0.三個值都不小於 0。
Total總和\(1.00\)\(0.70+0.20+0.10=1\).\(0.70+0.20+0.10=1\)。

The plain case is a probability table. Check nonnegative values and total 1.最基本的檢查就是看機率表：每個值要非負，總和要等於 1。

Example 2 · Using the complement rule範例 2 · 使用補集律

A device passes final test with probability 0.96. Find the probability that it fails final test.某裝置通過最終測試的機率為 0.96。求未通過最終測試的機率。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

\(P(\overline{A})\)\(0.04\)Use \(1-0.96\).用 \(1-0.96\)。

The new wrinkle is that the target is easier to find by subtracting from the whole sample space.這題的重點是：目標事件可以用「全部扣掉已知事件」更快算出來。

Example 3 · Independent-looking alarms are still events (engineering)範例 3 · 警報仍然是事件（工程應用）

In one production minute, alarm \(A\) fires with probability 0.18, alarm \(B\) fires with probability 0.12, and both fire with probability 0.05. Find \(P(A\cup B)\).在生產線的一分鐘內，警報 \(A\) 觸發機率為 0.18，警報 \(B\) 觸發機率為 0.12，兩者同時觸發機率為 0.05。求 \(P(A\cup B)\)。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量Value值How you read it判讀方式
\(P(A)\)\(0.18\)Given by the alarm log.由警報紀錄提供。
\(P(B)\)\(0.12\)Given by the alarm log.由警報紀錄提供。
\(P(A\cap B)\)\(0.05\)The overlap is counted in both \(P(A)\) and \(P(B)\).重疊區同時被計入 \(P(A)\) 與 \(P(B)\)。
\(P(A\cup B)\)\(0.25\)Use \(0.18+0.12-0.05\).用 \(0.18+0.12-0.05\)。

The engineering message is simple: subtract the overlap before interpreting "at least one alarm".工程上的重點很簡單：解讀「至少一個警報」之前，要先扣除重疊。

3 · Exercises3 · 練習

Exercise 1 · Check a model練習 1 · 檢查模型

A model assigns probabilities \(0.50, 0.30, 0.25\) to three disjoint outcomes that are supposed to cover \(S\).某模型把三個互斥、且應該涵蓋 \(S\) 的結果機率設成 \(0.50, 0.30, 0.25\)。

Answer all five parts:回答以下五個小題：

  1. Are the probabilities nonnegative?這些機率都非負嗎？
  2. What is their sum?它們的總和是多少？
  3. Is the model valid?這個模型有效嗎？
  4. Which axiom fails, if any?如果模型不合格，是哪一條公理出問題？
  5. How could one value be adjusted to make the total 1?如果只調整其中一個值，要怎麼讓總和變成 1？
Hint:提示： Disjoint outcomes that cover \(S\) must have probabilities that sum to 1.互斥且涵蓋 \(S\) 的結果，其機率總和必須為 1。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

(1) Yes, all three are nonnegative.(1) 是，三者都非負。

\[ 0.50+0.30+0.25=1.05. \]

(3) The model is not valid because the total is larger than 1.(3) 此模型無效，因為總和大於 1。

(4) The normalization axiom \(P(S)=1\) fails.(4) 正規化公理 \(P(S)=1\) 失敗。

(5) For example, change \(0.25\) to \(0.20\).(5) 例如把 \(0.25\) 改成 \(0.20\)。

Sense check. A probability model cannot promise more than the whole sample space.合理性檢查。機率模型的總量不能超過整個樣本空間。

Exercise 2 · Union from overlapping tests練習 2 · 由重疊測試求聯集

Two board failures satisfy \(P(A)=0.22\), \(P(B)=0.18\), and \(P(A\cap B)=0.06\).兩種板子故障事件滿足 \(P(A)=0.22\)、\(P(B)=0.18\)、\(P(A\cap B)=0.06\)。

Answer all five parts:回答以下五個小題：

  1. Write the general addition law.寫出一般加法律。
  2. Compute \(P(A\cup B)\).計算 \(P(A\cup B)\)。
  3. Find \(P(\overline{A\cup B})\).求 \(P(\overline{A\cup B})\)。
  4. Explain why adding \(0.22+0.18\) is too large.說明為什麼直接加 \(0.22+0.18\) 會太大。
  5. Check that all final probabilities are in \([0,1]\).檢查所有最終機率都在 \([0,1]\)。
Hint:提示： The overlap appears inside both \(P(A)\) and \(P(B)\).重疊區同時出現在 \(P(A)\) 與 \(P(B)\) 中。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

\[ P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(A\cap B). \]

\[ P(A\cup B)=0.22+0.18-0.06=0.34. \]

\[ P(\overline{A\cup B})=1-0.34=0.66. \]

(4) Adding \(0.22+0.18\) counts the \(0.06\) overlap twice.(4) 直接加 \(0.22+0.18\) 會把 \(0.06\) 的重疊區算兩次。

(5) Both \(0.34\) and \(0.66\) are between 0 and 1.(5) \(0.34\) 與 \(0.66\) 都介於 0 與 1 之間。

Sense check. The union \(0.34\) is at least as large as either event alone, but less than their raw sum \(0.40\).合理性檢查。聯集 \(0.34\) 至少不小於任一單一事件，但小於直接相加的 \(0.40\)。
