2.3 Equally Likely Outcomes2.3 等可能結果

1 · Concept and Fundamental Knowledge1 · 概念與基礎知識

Why an electronic engineer needs this. Random test vectors, scrambled data, and randomized trials are often designed so that outcomes are equally likely. Then a probability question becomes a counting question.電子工程師為什麼需要這個。隨機測試向量、擾碼資料與隨機化試驗常常刻意設計成等可能。這時候機率問題就會回到「怎麼數」。

Definition · equally likely outcomes定義 · 等可能結果

Outcomes in a finite sample space \(S\) are equally likely when every single outcome has probability \(1/\lvert S\rvert\). In that case, events are measured by counting outcomes.有限樣本空間 \(S\) 中，若每個單一結果的機率都是 \(1/\lvert S\rvert\)，就稱為等可能結果。這時事件機率就可以用計數算出來。

Key terms · favourable and total重要名詞 · 有利與總數

  • Total outcomes. \(\lvert S\rvert\), the size of the sample space.總結果數。\(\lvert S\rvert\)，也就是樣本空間大小。
  • Favourable outcomes. \(\lvert A\rvert\), the number of outcomes that make event \(A\) happen.有利結果數。\(\lvert A\rvert\)，也就是使事件 \(A\) 發生的結果數。
  • Uniform model. A model where all outcomes in \(S\) are equally likely.均勻模型。樣本空間 \(S\) 中所有結果等可能的模型。

Simulate it first先模擬看看

Predict: if two fair dice are rolled, what fraction of trials have sum 7?預測：若擲兩顆公正骰子，點數和為 7 的比例會是多少？

import numpy as np
rng = np.random.default_rng(0)

d1 = rng.integers(1, 7, size=200_000)
d2 = rng.integers(1, 7, size=200_000)
event = (d1 + d2 == 7)
print(event.mean())

Observed: about \(0.167\), matching \(6/36=1/6\). The sample space has 36 ordered pairs.觀察結果：約 \(0.167\)，符合 \(6/36=1/6\)。樣本空間有 36 個有序對。

Rule · counting probability規則 · 計數機率

\[ P(A)=\frac{\lvert A\rvert}{\lvert S\rvert}\qquad\text{when all outcomes in }S\text{ are equally likely}. \]

The formula is powerful but conditional: it only works after you have a sample space whose outcomes are equally likely.這個公式很好用，但有前提：必須先確認樣本空間中的每個結果都等可能。

2 · Worked Examples2 · 範例（詳解）

Example 1 · One fair die範例 1 · 一顆公正骰子

Roll one fair die. Find the probability of rolling at least 5.擲一顆公正骰子。求擲出至少 5 點的機率。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量Value值How you read it判讀方式
\(S\)\(\{1,2,3,4,5,6\}\)The six die faces are equally likely.六個骰面等可能。
\(A\)\(\{5,6\}\)At least 5 means 5 or 6.至少 5 表示 5 或 6。
\(P(A)\)\(2/6=1/3\)Two favourable outcomes out of six.六個結果中有兩個有利。

The plain case shows the full method: count favourable outcomes and divide by total outcomes.最基本的情況就是完整流程：先數有利結果，再除以總結果數。

Example 2 · Sum of two dice範例 2 · 兩顆骰子的點數和

Roll two fair dice. Find the probability that the sum is 7.擲兩顆公正骰子。求點數和為 7 的機率。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量Value值How you read it判讀方式
\(\lvert S\rvert\)\(36\)Two ordered dice give \(6\cdot 6\) outcomes.兩顆骰子要看成有順序的對，所以有 \(6\cdot 6\) 個結果。
\(\lvert A\rvert\)\(6\)The highlighted pairs are \((1,6),(2,5),\ldots,(6,1)\).著色的有序對是 \((1,6),(2,5),\ldots,(6,1)\)。
\(P(A)\)\(6/36=1/6\)Six favourable pairs out of thirty-six.三十六個對中有六個有利。

The new wrinkle is that the sample space uses ordered pairs, not the unordered sums.這題的重點是樣本空間要用有序對，不能只看點數和本身。

Example 3 · Exactly two high bits in a byte (engineering)範例 3 · 位元組中恰有兩個高位元（工程應用）

A random 8-bit byte is equally likely to be any of the \(2^8\) patterns. Find the probability that exactly two bits are 1.一個隨機 8 位元位元組，等可能落在 \(2^8\) 種樣式之一。求恰有兩個位元為 1 的機率。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量Value值How you read it判讀方式
\(\lvert S\rvert\)\(2^8=256\)Each of 8 bits has two choices.8 個位元各有兩種選擇。
\(\lvert A\rvert\)\(\binom{8}{2}=28\)Choose which two bit positions contain 1.選出哪兩個位元位置為 1。
\(P(A)\)\(28/256=7/64\)Favourable byte patterns over all byte patterns.有利的位元組樣式數，除以所有位元組樣式數。

The engineering application combines counting and probability: first choose positions, then divide by all possible bytes.這個工程例子把計數和機率接在一起：先數出 1 的位置選法，再除以所有可能的位元組。

3 · Exercises3 · 練習

Exercise 1 · A random card nibble練習 1 · 隨機四位元半位元組

A 4-bit nibble is chosen uniformly from all \(2^4\) bit patterns. Find the probability that exactly one bit is 1.一個 4 位元半位元組會從所有 \(2^4\) 種位元樣式中等可能選取。求恰有一個位元為 1 的機率。

Answer all five parts:回答以下五個小題：

  1. Find \(\lvert S\rvert\).求 \(\lvert S\rvert\)。
  2. Count the patterns with exactly one 1.計算恰有一個 1 的樣式數。
  3. Find the probability.求機率。
  4. List the favourable patterns.列出有利樣式。
  5. Explain why the outcomes are equally likely.說明為什麼結果等可能。
Hint:提示： Exactly one bit is 1 means choose the position of the single 1.恰有一個位元為 1，就是在四個位置中選出唯一放 1 的位置。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

\[ \lvert S\rvert=2^4=16. \]

\[ \lvert A\rvert=\binom{4}{1}=4. \]

\[ P(A)=\frac{4}{16}=\frac14. \]

(4) The favourable patterns are \(1000,0100,0010,0001\).(4) 有利樣式為 \(1000,0100,0010,0001\)。

(5) The model says the nibble is chosen uniformly from all 16 patterns, so each pattern has probability \(1/16\).(5) 題目設定半位元組會在 16 種樣式中均勻選取，因此每個樣式機率為 \(1/16\)。

Sense check. Four patterns out of sixteen gives \(1/4\), a probability between 0 and 1.合理性檢查。十六個樣式中有四個，得到 \(1/4\)，介於 0 與 1 之間。

Exercise 2 · At least one failed module練習 2 · 至少一個模組失效

Three independent-looking modules are each recorded simply as pass \(P\) or fail \(F\). Suppose the eight strings \(PPP,\ldots,FFF\) are equally likely. Find the probability of at least one fail.三個模組各自只記錄成通過 \(P\) 或失效 \(F\)。假設八個字串 \(PPP,\ldots,FFF\) 等可能。求至少一個失效的機率。

Answer all five parts:回答以下五個小題：

  1. Find \(\lvert S\rvert\).求 \(\lvert S\rvert\)。
  2. Find the complement of "at least one fail".找出「至少一個失效」的補集。
  3. Find the complement probability.求補集機率。
  4. Find the target probability.求目標機率。
  5. Explain why complement counting is faster here.說明為什麼這裡用補集計數比較快。
Hint:提示： "At least one" is often easiest as \(1-\) "none".遇到「至少一個」，常常用 \(1-\)「一個都沒有」最快。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

\[ \lvert S\rvert=2^3=8. \]

(2) The complement is no fail, namely \(PPP\).(2) 補集是完全沒有失效，也就是 \(PPP\)。

\[ P(PPP)=\frac{1}{8}. \]

\[ P(\text{at least one fail})=1-\frac18=\frac78. \]

(5) Counting the complement needs only one string, while direct counting needs seven strings.(5) 補集只要數一個字串；直接數則要列出七個字串。

Sense check. At least one fail covers most of the eight equally likely strings, so \(7/8\) is plausible.合理性檢查。八個等可能字串中，除了 \(PPP\) 之外都至少有一個失效，所以 \(7/8\) 合理。
