7.1 Reliability and Manufacturing Quality7.1 可靠度與製造品質

1 · Concept and Fundamental Knowledge1 · 概念與基礎知識

Why an electronic engineer needs this. Reliability and quality turn field returns, burn-in tests, and production sampling into measurable probabilities. The same formulas help you ask whether a product is likely to survive its warranty and whether a batch should be accepted.電子工程師為什麼需要這個。可靠度與品質把現場退貨、燒機測試與生產抽樣轉成可量測的機率。同一套公式可以回答產品是否可能撐過保固，以及一批產品是否應該放行。

Definition · reliability and defect count定義 · 可靠度與不良品數

The reliability function is \(R(t)=P(T\gt t)\), the probability a lifetime \(T\) is still operating after time \(t\). A defect count \(X\) records how many units in a sample fail inspection.可靠度函數為 \(R(t)=P(T\gt t)\)，表示壽命 \(T\) 超過時間 \(t\) 仍可運作的機率。不良品數 \(X\) 記錄樣本中有幾個單位未通過檢驗。

Simulate it first先模擬看看

Predict: if average lifetime is 2000 hours, what fraction of components survive beyond 1000 hours?預測：如果平均壽命為 2000 小時，超過 1000 小時仍可運作的比例大約是多少？

import numpy as np
rng = np.random.default_rng(7)

lifetimes = rng.exponential(scale=2000, size=200_000)
survive_1000 = lifetimes > 1000
print(survive_1000.mean())

Observed: about \(0.607\), matching \(e^{-1000/2000}\).觀察結果：約 \(0.607\)，符合 \(e^{-1000/2000}\)。

Key terms · reliability sampling重要名詞 · 可靠度抽樣

  • Lifetime \(T\). A continuous random variable measured in hours, cycles, or days.壽命 \(T\)。以小時、循環次數或天數量測的連續隨機變數。
  • Failure rate \(\lambda\). For an exponential model, the mean lifetime is \(1/\lambda\).故障率 \(\lambda\)。在指數模型中，平均壽命為 \(1/\lambda\)。
  • Defect probability \(p\). The probability one inspected unit is defective.不良率 \(p\)。單一受檢單位為不良品的機率。

Rule · exponential survival and binomial defects規則 · 指數存活與二項不良品數

\[ R(t)=P(T\gt t)=e^{-\lambda t},\qquad P(X=k)=\binom{n}{k}p^k(1-p)^{n-k}. \]

Use the exponential model for memoryless time to failure. Use the binomial model for the number of defectives in \(n\) independent inspections.記憶性不重要的故障等待時間可用指數模型。\(n\) 個獨立檢驗中的不良品數可用二項模型。

2 · Worked Examples2 · 範例（詳解）

Example 1 · Survival past warranty time範例 1 · 撐過保固時間

A capacitor lifetime is exponential with mean 4000 hours. Find the probability it survives beyond 1000 hours.某電容壽命服從平均 4000 小時的指數分布。求超過 1000 小時仍可運作的機率。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量Value值How you read it判讀方式
\(T\)lifetime壽命The random variable is operating time.隨機變數是運作時間。
\(\lambda\)\(1/4000\)The mean of an exponential is \(1/\lambda\).指數分布平均值為 \(1/\lambda\)。
\(R(1000)\)\(e^{-1000/4000}=0.779\)Read survival as area to the right of 1000.存活機率是 1000 右側的面積。

The plain case is direct survival: plug the time into \(R(t)\).基本題型是直接求存活機率：把時間代入 \(R(t)\)。

Example 2 · At most one defective board範例 2 · 最多一片不良板

A board has defect probability \(p=0.03\). In a sample of \(n=20\), find \(P(X\le 1)\).某電路板不良率 \(p=0.03\)。抽樣 \(n=20\) 片，求 \(P(X\le 1)\)。

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量Value值How you read it判讀方式
\(X\)\(\operatorname{Bin}(20,0.03)\)Count defectives among 20 independent boards.計算 20 片獨立電路板中的不良品數。
\(P(X=0)\)\(0.97^{20}\)No defective boards.沒有不良板。
\(P(X\le 1)\)\(0.97^{20}+20(0.03)0.97^{19}=0.880\)Add the bars for 0 and 1 defect.把 0 片與 1 片不良的長條相加。

The wrinkle is cumulative probability, so more than one bar may be included.這題多了累積機率，因此可能要加總多個長條。

Example 3 · Burn-in screen decision (engineering)範例 3 · 燒機篩選決策（工程應用）

A module must have at least 0.90 chance to survive 500 hours. Its exponential mean is 6000 hours. Does it pass the screen?某模組必須有至少 0.90 的機率撐過 500 小時。其指數平均壽命為 6000 小時。它是否通過篩選？

Reading it off, step by step逐步判讀

Quantity量Value值How you read it判讀方式
\(\lambda\)\(1/6000\)Convert the mean lifetime to a rate.把平均壽命轉成故障率。
\(R(500)\)\(e^{-500/6000}=0.920\)Survival through the burn-in target.撐過燒機目標時間的機率。
Decision決策pass通過\(0.920\gt 0.90\).因為 \(0.920\gt 0.90\)。

Engineering use means translating a probability into an acceptance decision.工程應用的重點，是把機率轉成是否接受的決策。

3 · Exercises3 · 練習

Exercise 1 · Battery survival練習 1 · 電池存活

A battery lifetime is exponential with mean 1200 cycles.某電池壽命服從平均 1200 次循環的指數分布。

  1. Write \(\lambda\).寫出 \(\lambda\)。
  2. Find \(P(T\gt 600)\).求 \(P(T\gt 600)\)。
  3. Interpret the answer in one sentence.用一句話解釋答案。
Hint:提示： Use \(R(t)=e^{-\lambda t}\) and \(\lambda=1/\text{mean}\).使用 \(R(t)=e^{-\lambda t}\)，且 \(\lambda=1/\text{平均值}\)。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

The mean is 1200 cycles, so the rate is:平均值為 1200 次循環，因此故障率為：

\[ \lambda=\frac{1}{1200}. \]

\[ P(T\gt 600)=e^{-600/1200}=e^{-0.5}=0.607. \]

Sense check. Since 600 is half the mean lifetime, survival should be above 0.5, and 0.607 is reasonable.合理性檢查。600 是平均壽命的一半，存活機率應大於 0.5，因此 0.607 合理。

Exercise 2 · Acceptance sampling練習 2 · 驗收抽樣

A lot is sampled with \(n=15\) units and defect probability \(p=0.04\). Accept the lot if \(X\le 1\).某批產品抽驗 \(n=15\) 件，不良率 \(p=0.04\)。若 \(X\le 1\) 則接受該批。

  1. Name the distribution of \(X\).寫出 \(X\) 的分布。
  2. Compute \(P(X\le 1)\).計算 \(P(X\le 1)\)。
  3. Explain why \(P(X=0)+P(X=1)\) is needed.說明為何需要 \(P(X=0)+P(X=1)\)。
Hint:提示： The acceptance rule includes both zero and one defective unit.驗收規則同時包含 0 件與 1 件不良品。
Show full solution顯示完整解答

The count is binomial because each unit is either good or defective:因為每件產品不是良品就是不良品，所以計數為二項分布：

\[ X\sim\operatorname{Bin}(15,0.04). \]

\[ P(X\le 1)=0.96^{15}+15(0.04)(0.96)^{14}=0.881. \]

Both \(X=0\) and \(X=1\) satisfy the acceptance rule, so both terms are included.\(X=0\) 與 \(X=1\) 都符合驗收規則，所以兩項都要納入。

Sense check. The defect rate is small, so a high acceptance probability is expected.合理性檢查。不良率很低，因此驗收機率偏高是合理的。
